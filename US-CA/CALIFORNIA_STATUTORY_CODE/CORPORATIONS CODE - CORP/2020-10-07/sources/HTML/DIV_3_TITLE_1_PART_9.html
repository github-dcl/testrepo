Corporations Code - CORP

TITLE 1. CORPORATIONS [100 - 14631]

( Title 1 enacted by Stats. 1947, Ch. 1038. )

DIVISION 3. CORPORATIONS FOR SPECIFIC PURPOSES [12000 - 14631]

( Division 3 enacted by Stats. 1947, Ch. 1038. )

PART 9. CABLE TELEVISION CORPORATIONS [14400- 14400.]
( Part 9 added by Stats. 1997, Ch. 598, Sec. 5. )

14400.

Any person who willfully and maliciously does any injury to any property of a cable television corporation is liable to the corporation for three times the amount of actual damages sustained thereby, to be recovered in any court of competent jurisdiction.

(Added by Stats. 1997, Ch. 598, Sec. 5. Effective January 1, 1998.)
