TITLE 1 GENERAL EDUCATION CODE PROVISIONS [1. - 32500]

( Title 1 enacted by Stats. 1976, Ch. 1010. )

DIVISION 1 GENERAL EDUCATION CODE PROVISIONS [1. - 32500]

( Division 1 enacted by Stats. 1976, Ch. 1010. )

PART 1 GENERAL PROVISIONS [1. - 446]

( Part 1 enacted by Stats. 1976, Ch. 1010. )

CHAPTER 2. Educational Equity [200 - 262.4]

( Heading of Chapter 2 amended by Stats. 1998, Ch. 914, Sec. 6. )

ARTICLE 6. Alternative Schools, Charter Schools, and School Choice [235- 235.]
( Article 6 heading added by Stats. 1998, Ch. 914, Sec. 27. )

235.

There shall be no discrimination on the basis of the characteristics listed in Section 220 in any aspect of the operation of alternative schools or charter schools.

(Amended by Stats. 2007, Ch. 569, Sec. 13. Effective January 1, 2008.)
