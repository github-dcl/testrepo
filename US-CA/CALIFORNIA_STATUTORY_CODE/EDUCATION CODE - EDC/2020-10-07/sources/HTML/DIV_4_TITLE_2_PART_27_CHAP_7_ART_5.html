ARTICLE 5. Compliance [49151- 49151.]
  ( Article 5 enacted by Stats. 1976, Ch. 1010. )

49151.
  

Nothing in this chapter shall be construed to repeal or in any way modify the provisions of Sections 1298, 1390, 1394, 1396, and 1397 of the Labor Code.
