DIVISION 7. COMMUNITY COLLEGES [70900 - 88933]

PART 50. FINANCE [84000 - 85304]

  ( Part 50 enacted by Stats. 1976, Ch. 1010. )

CHAPTER 5. Community College Apportionment [84750.5 - 84920]

  ( Chapter 5 repealed and added by Stats. 1979, Ch. 282. )

ARTICLE 2.5. Adjustments to Lottery Fund Allocations [84820- 84820.]
  ( Article 2.5 added by Stats. 1992, Ch. 454, Sec. 6. )

84820.
  

Any lottery revenue allocated to a public education entity pursuant to Section 8880.5 of the Government Code shall not be reduced by more than one-half of 1 percent per year when that reduction is the direct result of any entity changing its method of computing workload units.

(Amended by Stats. 1995, Ch. 758, Sec. 129. Effective January 1, 1996.)
