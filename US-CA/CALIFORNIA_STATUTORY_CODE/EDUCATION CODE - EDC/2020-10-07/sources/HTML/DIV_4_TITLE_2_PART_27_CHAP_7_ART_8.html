Education Code - EDC

TITLE 2. ELEMENTARY AND SECONDARY EDUCATION [33000 - 64100]

( Title 2 enacted by Stats. 1976, Ch. 1010. )

DIVISION 4. INSTRUCTION AND SERVICES [46000 - 65001]

( Division 4 enacted by Stats. 1976, Ch. 1010. )

PART 27. PUPILS [48000 - 49703]

( Part 27 enacted by Stats. 1976, Ch. 1010. )

CHAPTER 7. Employment of Minors [49100 - 49200]

( Chapter 7 enacted by Stats. 1976, Ch. 1010. )

ARTICLE 8. Permit to Work during Extended Emergency School Closure [49200- 49200.]
( Article 8 added by Stats. 2020, Ch. 64, Sec. 3. )

49200.

(a)Documentation required pursuant to this chapter, including signatures, may be collected electronically during an extended school closure due to a natural disaster, pandemic, or other emergency.

(b)A work permit shall not be denied based on a pupils grades, grade point average, or school attendance when the pupils school has been physically closed for an extended time due to a natural disaster, pandemic, or other emergency.

(Added by Stats. 2020, Ch. 64, Sec. 3. (AB 908) Effective September 11, 2020.)
