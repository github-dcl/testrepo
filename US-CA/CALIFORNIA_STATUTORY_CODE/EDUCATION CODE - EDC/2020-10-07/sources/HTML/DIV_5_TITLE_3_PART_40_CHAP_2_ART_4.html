PART 40. DONAHOE HIGHER EDUCATION ACT [66000 - 67400]

  ( Part 40 enacted by Stats. 1976, Ch. 1010. )

CHAPTER 2. General Provisions [66010 - 66093.4]

  ( Chapter 2 enacted by Stats. 1976, Ch. 1010. )

ARTICLE 4. Educational Equity for Students [66030- 66030.]
  ( Article 4 added by Stats. 1991, Ch. 1198, Sec. 9. )

66030.
  

(a) It is the intent of the Legislature that public higher education in California strive to provide educationally equitable environments that give each Californian, regardless of age, economic circumstance, or the characteristics listed in Section 66270, a reasonable opportunity to develop fully his or her potential.

(b) It is the responsibility of the governing boards of institutions of higher education to ensure and maintain multicultural learning environments free from all forms of discrimination and harassment, in accordance with state and federal law.

(Amended by Stats. 2007, Ch. 569, Sec. 32. Effective January 1, 2008.)
