Education Code - EDC

TITLE 2. ELEMENTARY AND SECONDARY EDUCATION [33000 - 64100]

( Title 2 enacted by Stats. 1976, Ch. 1010. )

DIVISION 4. INSTRUCTION AND SERVICES [46000 - 65001]

( Division 4 enacted by Stats. 1976, Ch. 1010. )

PART 28. GENERAL INSTRUCTIONAL PROGRAMS [51000 - 53315]

( Part 28 enacted by Stats. 1976, Ch. 1010. )

CHAPTER 5. Authorized Classes and Courses of Instruction [51705 - 51879.9]

( Chapter 5 enacted by Stats. 1976, Ch. 1010. )

ARTICLE 6. Military Science and Tactics [51750- 51750.]
( Article 6 enacted by Stats. 1976, Ch. 1010. )

51750.

The governing board of any school district maintaining a secondary school may establish in the school courses in military science and tactics complying with the laws of the United States made and provided with reference to Reserve Officers Training Corps units in educational institutions.

No student enrolled in any such school shall be required to enroll in any course in military science and tactics.

(Enacted by Stats. 1976, Ch. 1010.)
