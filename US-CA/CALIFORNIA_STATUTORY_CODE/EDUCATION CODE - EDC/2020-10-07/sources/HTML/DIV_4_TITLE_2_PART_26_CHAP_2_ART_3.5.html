ARTICLE 3.5. State High School Attendance Awards [46150- 46150.]
  ( Article 3.5 added by Stats. 1984, Ch. 36, Sec. 1. )

46150.
  

It is the intent of the Legislature that efforts by high school faculty, staff, and students to improve student attendance at their schools be recognized. It is also the intent of the Legislature that this recognition shall be provided through a state high school attendance awards program, if feasible.

(Added by Stats. 1984, Ch. 36, Sec. 1.)
