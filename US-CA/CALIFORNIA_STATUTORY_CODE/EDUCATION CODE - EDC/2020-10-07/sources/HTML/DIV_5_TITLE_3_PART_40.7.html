Education Code - EDC

TITLE 3. POSTSECONDARY EDUCATION [66000 - 101060]

( Title 3 enacted by Stats. 1976, Ch. 1010. )

DIVISION 5. GENERAL PROVISIONS [66000 - 70115.2]

( Division 5 enacted by Stats. 1976, Ch. 1010. )

PART 40.7. ARMED FORCES TRAINING AND RECRUITMENT [67600- 67600.]
( Part 40.7 added by Stats. 1995, Ch. 398, Sec. 1. )

67600.

Nothing in the public policy of the State of California requires the establishment or the disestablishment of any United States Armed Forces training unit or class at any campus of a public postsecondary educational institution or the prohibition of employment recruitment by the United States Armed Forces at any campus of a public postsecondary educational institution.

(Added by Stats. 1995, Ch. 398, Sec. 1. Effective January 1, 1996.)
