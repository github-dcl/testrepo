Water Code - WAT

DIVISION 16. COUNTY WATERWORKS DISTRICTS [55000 - 55991]

  ( Division 16 added by Stats. 1953, Ch. 155. )

PART 4. FINANCIAL PROVISIONS [55500 - 55720]

  ( Part 4 added by Stats. 1953, Ch. 155. )

CHAPTER 2. Bonds [55520 - 55679]

  ( Chapter 2 added by Stats. 1953, Ch. 155. )

ARTICLE 5. Validating Proceedings [55630- 55630.]
  ( Article 5 repealed and added by Stats. 1961, Ch. 1496. )

55630.
  

An action to determine the validity of bonds may be brought pursuant to Chapter 9 (commencing with Section 860) of Title 10 of Part 2 of the Code of Civil Procedure.

(Repealed and added by Stats. 1961, Ch. 1496.)
