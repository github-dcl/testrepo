Water Code - WAT

DIVISION 11. IRRIGATION DISTRICTS [20500 - 29978]

  ( Division 11 added by Stats. 1943, Ch. 372. )

PART 9. BONDS [24950 - 25402]

  ( Part 9 added by Stats. 1943, Ch. 372. )

CHAPTER 2. Refunding Bonds [25035 - 25119]

  ( Chapter 2 added by Stats. 1943, Ch. 372. )

ARTICLE 3. Authorized Refunding Modification [25091 - 25092]
  ( Article 3 added by Stats. 1943, Ch. 372. )

25091.
  

The board shall call an election for the purpose of authorizing the modification of a refunding plan.

(Amended by Stats. 1991, Ch. 1226, Sec. 54.)

25092.
  

Notice of the election shall be given and it shall be held and its result determined and declared substantially in the same manner as a bond election, except that a majority vote only is required for the approval of the modification.

(Added by Stats. 1943, Ch. 372.)
