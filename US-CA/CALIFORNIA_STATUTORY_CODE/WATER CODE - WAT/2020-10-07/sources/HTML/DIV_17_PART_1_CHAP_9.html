Water Code - WAT

DIVISION 17. DRAINAGE [56000 - 56130]

  ( Division 17 added by Stats. 1955, Ch. 1232. )

PART 1. COUNTY DRAINAGE DISTRICTS [56000 - 56130]

  ( Part 1 added by Stats. 1955, Ch. 1232. )

CHAPTER 9. Annexation [56130- 56130.]
  ( Chapter 9 added by Stats. 1955, Ch. 1232. )

56130.
  

Territory, whether incorporated or unincorporated, contiguous to a district, and not included in any other county drainage district or other district formed for similar purposes, may be annexed, if the additional territory will be benefited by annexation.

(Amended by Stats. 1965, Ch. 2043.)
