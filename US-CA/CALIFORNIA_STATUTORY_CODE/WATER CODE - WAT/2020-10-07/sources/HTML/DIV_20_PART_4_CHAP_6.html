Water Code - WAT

DIVISION 20. MUNICIPAL WATER DISTRICTS [71000 - 73001]

( Division 20 added by Stats. 1963, Ch. 156. )

PART 4. ELECTIONS [71450 - 71540]

( Part 4 added by Stats. 1963, Ch. 156. )

CHAPTER 6. Relocation of Division Boundaries [71540- 71540.]
( Chapter 6 added by Stats. 1963, Ch. 156. )

71540.

The board of directors shall, by resolution, adjust the boundaries of any divisions pursuant to Chapter 8 (commencing with Section 22000) of Division 21 of the Elections Code.

(Repealed and added by Stats. 1998, Ch. 435, Sec. 14. Effective January 1, 1999.)
