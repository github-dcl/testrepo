DIVISION 15. RECLAMATION DISTRICTS [50000 - 53901]

( Division 15 added by Stats. 1951, Ch. 336. )

PART 8. BONDS AND REFUNDING BONDS [52100 - 52862]

( Part 8 added by Stats. 1951, Ch. 336. )

CHAPTER 1. General Provisions [52100 - 52193]

( Chapter 1 added by Stats. 1951, Ch. 336. )

ARTICLE 2. Validating Proceedings [52120- 52120.]
( Article 2 repealed and added by Stats. 1961, Ch. 1543. )

52120.

An action to determine the validity of bonds may be brought pursuant to Chapter 9 (commencing with Section 860) of Title 10 of Part 2 of the Code of Civil Procedure.

(Repealed and added by Stats. 1961, Ch. 1543.)
