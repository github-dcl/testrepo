Water Code - WAT

DIVISION 11. IRRIGATION DISTRICTS [20500 - 29978]

( Division 11 added by Stats. 1943, Ch. 372. )

PART 11. CHANGES IN ORGANIZATION [26875- 26875.]

( Part 11 added by Stats. 1943, Ch. 372. )

CHAPTER 2. Inclusion [26875- 26875.]

( Chapter 2 added by Stats. 1943, Ch. 372. )

ARTICLE 1. Petition and Notice [26875- 26875.]
( Article 1 added by Stats. 1943, Ch. 372. )

26875.

Land which is not a part of the district, whether or not contiguous to it, may be included within the district.

If such land is not capable of being served water at the time of inclusion, the board may exempt the included parcel from the imposition of district assessments, in whole or in part, until such time as a water supply is available for service to such land.

(Amended by Stats. 1971, Ch. 817.)
