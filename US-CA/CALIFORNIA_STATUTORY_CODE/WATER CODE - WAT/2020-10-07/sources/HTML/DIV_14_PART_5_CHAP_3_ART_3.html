Water Code - WAT

DIVISION 14. CALIFORNIA WATER STORAGE DISTRICT LAW [39000 - 48401]

  ( Division 14 added by Stats. 1951, Ch. 391. )

PART 5. DISTRICT PROJECT [42200 - 42752]

  ( Part 5 added by Stats. 1951, Ch. 391. )

CHAPTER 3. Recommendation of Completion of Project [42525 - 42552]

  ( Chapter 3 added by Stats. 1951, Ch. 391. )

ARTICLE 3. Proceedings After Election [42550 - 42552]
  ( Article 3 added by Stats. 1951, Ch. 391. )

42550.
  

If a majority of all votes cast are “Completion of project—Yes” and also a majority of the qualified voters who voted at the election voted “Completion of project—Yes,” the report and recommendation is adopted.

(Added by Stats. 1951, Ch. 391.)

42551.
  

The board shall carry out the project of the district in accordance with the plans and specifications of the approved and adopted report of the board.

(Added by Stats. 1951, Ch. 391.)

42552.
  

If completion of the project is not approved at the election, the project is abandoned.

(Amended by Stats. 1992, Ch. 1020, Sec. 72. Effective January 1, 1993.)
