Water Code - WAT

DIVISION 6. CONSERVATION, DEVELOPMENT, AND UTILIZATION OF STATE WATER RESOURCES [10000 - 12999]

  ( Heading of Division 6 amended by Stats. 1957, Ch. 1932. )

PART 2.8. AGRICULTURAL WATER MANAGEMENT PLANNING [10800 - 10853]

  ( Repealed and added by Stats. 2009, 7th Ex. Sess., Ch. 4, Sec. 4. )

CHAPTER 2. Definitions [10810 - 10817]
  ( Chapter 2 added by Stats. 2009, 7th Ex. Sess., Ch. 4, Sec. 4. )

10810.
  

Unless the context otherwise requires, the definitions set forth in this chapter govern the construction of this part.

(Added by Stats. 2009, 7th Ex. Sess., Ch. 4, Sec. 4. (SB 7 7x) Effective February 3, 2010.)

10811.
  

“Agricultural water management plan” or “plan” means an agricultural water management plan prepared pursuant to this part.

(Added by Stats. 2009, 7th Ex. Sess., Ch. 4, Sec. 4. (SB 7 7x) Effective February 3, 2010.)

10812.
  

“Agricultural water supplier” has the same meaning as defined in Section 10608.12.

(Added by Stats. 2009, 7th Ex. Sess., Ch. 4, Sec. 4. (SB 7 7x) Effective February 3, 2010.)

10813.
  

“Customer” means a purchaser of water from a water supplier who uses water for agricultural purposes.

(Added by Stats. 2009, 7th Ex. Sess., Ch. 4, Sec. 4. (SB 7 7x) Effective February 3, 2010.)

10814.
  

“Person” has the same meaning as defined in Section 10614.

(Amended by Stats. 2018, Ch. 15, Sec. 11. (AB 1668) Effective January 1, 2019.)

10815.
  

“Public agency” means any city, county, city and county, special district, or other public entity.

(Added by Stats. 2009, 7th Ex. Sess., Ch. 4, Sec. 4. (SB 7 7x) Effective February 3, 2010.)

10816.
  

“Urban water supplier” has the same meaning as set forth in Section 10617.

(Added by Stats. 2009, 7th Ex. Sess., Ch. 4, Sec. 4. (SB 7 7x) Effective February 3, 2010.)

10817.
  

“Water use efficiency” means the efficient management of water resources for beneficial uses, preventing waste, or accomplishing additional benefits with the same amount of water.

(Amended by Stats. 2018, Ch. 15, Sec. 12. (AB 1668) Effective January 1, 2019.)
