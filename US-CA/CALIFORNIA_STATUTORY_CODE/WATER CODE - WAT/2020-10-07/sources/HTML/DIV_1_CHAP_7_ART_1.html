Water Code - WAT

DIVISION 1. GENERAL STATE POWERS OVER WATER [100 - 540]

  ( Division 1 enacted by Stats. 1943, Ch. 368. )

CHAPTER 7. Water Transfer [470 - 484]

  ( Chapter 7 added by Stats. 1986, Ch. 970, Sec. 1. )

ARTICLE 1. Short Title [470- 470.]
  ( Article 1 added by Stats. 1986, Ch. 970, Sec. 1. )

470.
  

This chapter shall be known as and may be cited as the Costa-Isenberg Water Transfer Act of 1986.

(Added by Stats. 1986, Ch. 970, Sec. 1.)
