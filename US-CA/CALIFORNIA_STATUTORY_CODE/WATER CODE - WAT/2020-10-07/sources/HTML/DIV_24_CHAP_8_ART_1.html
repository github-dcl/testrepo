Water Code - WAT

DIVISION 24. SAFE, CLEAN, RELIABLE WATER SUPPLY ACT [78500 - 78702]

  ( Division 24 added by Stats. 1996, Ch. 135, Sec. 1. )

CHAPTER 8. Flood Control and Prevention Program [78686 - 78686.12]

  ( Chapter 8 added by Stats. 1996, Ch. 135, Sec. 1. )

ARTICLE 1. Definitions [78686- 78686.]
  ( Article 1 added by Stats. 1996, Ch. 135, Sec. 1. )

78686.
  

Unless the context otherwise requires, as used in this chapter, “account” means the Flood Control and Prevention Account created by Section 78686.10.

(Added by Stats. 1996, Ch. 135, Sec. 1. Approved in Proposition 204 at the November 5, 1996, election.)
