Water Code - WAT

DIVISION 15. RECLAMATION DISTRICTS [50000 - 53901]

  ( Division 15 added by Stats. 1951, Ch. 336. )

PART 1. INTRODUCTORY PROVISIONS [50000 - 50170]

  ( Part 1 added by Stats. 1951, Ch. 336. )

CHAPTER 2. General Provisions [50100 - 50170]

  ( Chapter 2 added by Stats. 1951, Ch. 336. )

ARTICLE 6. Evidence of Value and Ownership [50160- 50160.]
  ( Article 6 added by Stats. 1951, Ch. 336. )

50160.
  

For all purposes relating to signing petitions and bylaws and voting, the last equalized tax roll for each county wherein any district land is situated is sufficient evidence of ownership and of value of lands in the district.

(Amended by Stats. 1963, Ch. 1763.)
