Water Code - WAT

DIVISION 21. WATER CONSERVATION DISTRICTS [74000 - 76501]

  ( Division 21 added by Stats. 1965, Ch. 75. )

PART 11. REPEALS [76500 - 76501]
  ( Part 11 added by Stats. 1965, Ch. 75. )

76500.
  

Chapter 166 of the Statutes of 1929 and Chapter 1020 of the Statutes of 1931 are repealed.

(Added by Stats. 1965, Ch. 75.)

76501.
  

The repeals effected by this part shall not be construed to deprive any person or entity of any substantial right which would have existed or hereafter exist had such repeal not been effected.

(Added by Stats. 1965, Ch. 75.)
