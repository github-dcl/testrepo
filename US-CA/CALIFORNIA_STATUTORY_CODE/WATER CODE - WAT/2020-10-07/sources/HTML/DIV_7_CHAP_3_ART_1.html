Water Code - WAT

DIVISION 7. WATER QUALITY [13000 - 16104]

( Division 7 repealed and added by Stats. 1969, Ch. 482. )

CHAPTER 3. State Water Quality Control [13100 - 13197.5]

( Chapter 3 added by Stats. 1969, Ch. 482. )

ARTICLE 1. State Water Resources Control Board [13100- 13100.]
( Article 1 added by Stats. 1969, Ch. 482. )

13100.

There is in the Resources Agency the State Water Resources Control Board and the California regional water quality control boards. The organization, membership, and some of the duties of the state board are provided for in Article 3 (commencing with Section 174) of Chapter 2 of Division 1 of this code.

(Amended by Stats. 1976, Ch. 596. Note: See this section as modified on July 17, 1991, in Governor's Reorganization Plan No. 1 of 1991.)
