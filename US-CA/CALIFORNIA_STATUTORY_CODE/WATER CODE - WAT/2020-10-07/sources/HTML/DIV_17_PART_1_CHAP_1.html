Water Code - WAT

DIVISION 17. DRAINAGE [56000 - 56130]

( Division 17 added by Stats. 1955, Ch. 1232. )

PART 1. COUNTY DRAINAGE DISTRICTS [56000 - 56130]

( Part 1 added by Stats. 1955, Ch. 1232. )

CHAPTER 1. General Provisions [56000 - 56003]
( Chapter 1 added by Stats. 1955, Ch. 1232. )

56000.

This part shall be known and cited as the County Drainage District Act.

(Added by Stats. 1955, Ch. 1232.)

56001.

District as used in this part means any county drainage district formed pursuant to this part or pursuant to any law which it supersedes.

(Added by Stats. 1955, Ch. 1232.)

56002.

District board as used in this part means the board of directors of a district.

(Added by Stats. 1955, Ch. 1232.)

56003.

Districts may be formed, maintained and governed in any county as provided in this part.

(Added by Stats. 1955, Ch. 1232.)
