Water Code - WAT

DIVISION 14. CALIFORNIA WATER STORAGE DISTRICT LAW [39000 - 48401]

  ( Division 14 added by Stats. 1951, Ch. 391. )

PART 5. DISTRICT PROJECT [42200 - 42752]

  ( Part 5 added by Stats. 1951, Ch. 391. )

CHAPTER 2. Recommendation of Abandonment of Project [42301 - 42361]

  ( Chapter 2 added by Stats. 1951, Ch. 391. )

ARTICLE 1. Investigation by the Board [42301- 42301.]
  ( Heading of Article 1 amended by Stats. 1992, Ch. 1020, Sec. 57.5. )

42301.
  

If the board recommends that the project be abandoned, the board may call a special election to be held in the district for the purpose of determining whether the recommendation of the board shall be adopted or rejected.

(Amended by Stats. 1992, Ch. 1020, Sec. 59. Effective January 1, 1993.)
