  ( Division 12 added by Stats. 1949, Ch. 274. )

PART 10. REPEALS [33900 - 33901]
  ( Part 10 added by Stats. 1949, Ch. 274. )

33900.
  

The following acts are hereby repealed:

YearCh. Pg.
1913: 592: 1049
1937: 469: 1410


(Added by Stats. 1949, Ch. 274.)

33901.
  

The repeals effected by this part shall not be construed to deprive any district or any person or other entity of any substantial right which would have existed or hereafter exist had such repeals not been effected.

(Added by Stats. 1949, Ch. 274.)
