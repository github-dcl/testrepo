Water Code - WAT

DIVISION 14. CALIFORNIA WATER STORAGE DISTRICT LAW [39000 - 48401]

( Division 14 added by Stats. 1951, Ch. 391. )

PART 3. INTERNAL ORGANIZATION [40300 - 40800]

( Part 3 added by Stats. 1951, Ch. 391. )

CHAPTER 2. Appointments to Fill Vacancies [40500- 40500.]
( Chapter 2 added by Stats. 1951, Ch. 391. )

40500.

A vacancy in the office of director shall be filled pursuant to Section 1780 of the Government Code.

(Amended by Stats. 1975, Ch. 1059.)
