Water Code - WAT

DIVISION 15. RECLAMATION DISTRICTS [50000 - 53901]

( Division 15 added by Stats. 1951, Ch. 336. )

PART 5. DISTRICT POWERS AND DUTIES [50900 - 50979]

( Part 5 added by Stats. 1951, Ch. 336. )

CHAPTER 4. Office, Records and Documents [50940 - 50942]
( Chapter 4 added by Stats. 1951, Ch. 336. )

50940.

Unless otherwise provided by law the district shall keep an office in the principal county or in a county contiguous thereto for the transaction of the business of the district.

(Added by Stats. 1951, Ch. 336.)

50941.

All district books, maps, papers, records, contracts and other documents shall be kept in the office and be open to inspection during all business hours by any person interested.

(Added by Stats. 1951, Ch. 336.)

50942.

A district may destroy a record pursuant to Chapter 7 (commencing with Section 60200) of Division 1 of Title 6 of the Government Code.

(Added by Stats. 2005, Ch. 158, Sec. 41. Effective January 1, 2006.)
