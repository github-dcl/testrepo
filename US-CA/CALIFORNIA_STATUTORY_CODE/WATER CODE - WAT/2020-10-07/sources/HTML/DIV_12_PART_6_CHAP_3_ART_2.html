Water Code - WAT

DIVISION 12. COUNTY WATER DISTRICTS [30000 - 33901]

( Division 12 added by Stats. 1949, Ch. 274. )

PART 6. FINANCIAL PROVISIONS [31300 - 31639]

( Part 6 added by Stats. 1949, Ch. 274. )

CHAPTER 3. Adoption of Improvement Acts [31500 - 31548]

( Chapter 3 added by Stats. 1949, Ch. 274. )

ARTICLE 2. Definition of Improvement Act Terms [31520 - 31529]
( Article 2 added by Stats. 1949, Ch. 274. )

31520.

As used in the laws referred to in this chapter the terms therefrom set forth in this article refer for the purposes of this chapter to that which this article states each respectively refers.

(Added by Stats. 1949, Ch. 274.)

31521.

City refers to county water district.

(Added by Stats. 1949, Ch. 274.)

31522.

City council or legislative body refers to the board of directors of the county water district.

(Added by Stats. 1949, Ch. 274.)

31523.

Mayor refers to the president of the board of directors of the county water district.

(Added by Stats. 1949, Ch. 274.)

31524.

Clerk refers to the secretary of the board of directors of the county water district.

(Added by Stats. 1949, Ch. 274.)

31525.

Council chambers refers to the place designated by the board of directors of the county water district for holding its regular meetings.

(Added by Stats. 1949, Ch. 274.)

31526.

Treasurer refers to the auditor of the county water district, unless the district elects to use the county treasurer as provided in Section 31305, in which case it is the county treasurer as ex officio treasurer of the district.

(Amended by Stats. 1949, Ch. 1318.)

31527.

Superintendent of streets or street superintendent and city engineer refer to the engineer of the county water district.

(Added by Stats. 1949, Ch. 274.)

31528.

Right of way refers to any parcel of land through which a right of way has been granted to the county water district for any purpose.

(Added by Stats. 1949, Ch. 274.)

31529.

All words relating to municipal officers and matters refer to the corresponding officers of county water districts and matters under this division.

(Added by Stats. 1949, Ch. 274.)
