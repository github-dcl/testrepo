Water Code - WAT

DIVISION 14. CALIFORNIA WATER STORAGE DISTRICT LAW [39000 - 48401]

( Division 14 added by Stats. 1951, Ch. 391. )

PART 9. ASSESSMENTS [46000 - 47982]

( Part 9 added by Stats. 1951, Ch. 391. )

CHAPTER 5. Collection of Original Assessments [46670 - 46797]

( Chapter 5 added by Stats. 1951, Ch. 391. )

ARTICLE 3. Penalty for Delinquency [46710- 46710.]
( Article 3 added by Stats. 1951, Ch. 391. )

46710.

If any installment remains unpaid at the expiration of 60 days from the date of the order, it becomes delinquent, together with the accrued interest thereon, and a penalty of 10 percent of the amount of the installment and interest shall be added and collected for the use of the district.

(Added by Stats. 1951, Ch. 391.)
