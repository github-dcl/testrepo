Water Code - WAT

DIVISION 19. LEVEE DISTRICTS [70000 - 70272]

( Division 19 added by Stats. 1959, Ch. 370. )

PART 1. LEVEE DISTRICT LAW OF 1959 [70000 - 70272]

( Part 1 added by Stats. 1959, Ch. 370. )

CHAPTER 1. General Provisions [70000 - 70001]
( Chapter 1 added by Stats. 1959, Ch. 370. )

70000.

This part shall be known and may be cited as the Levee District Law of 1959.

(Added by Stats. 1959, Ch. 370.)

70001.

Nothing in this part affects the provisions of any statute in relation to levee districts now in force.

(Added by Stats. 1959, Ch. 370.)
