Water Code - WAT

DIVISION 19. LEVEE DISTRICTS [70000 - 70272]

  ( Division 19 added by Stats. 1959, Ch. 370. )

PART 1. LEVEE DISTRICT LAW OF 1959 [70000 - 70272]

  ( Part 1 added by Stats. 1959, Ch. 370. )

CHAPTER 5. Powers and Purposes [70150 - 70151]

  ( Chapter 5 added by Stats. 1959, Ch. 370. )

ARTICLE 1. Powers Generally [70150 - 70151]
  ( Article 1 added by Stats. 1959, Ch. 370. )

70150.
  

The district may acquire by purchase, condemnation, gift or other action, drains, canals, sluices, bulkheads, watergates, levees, embankments, pumping plants and pipelines and to purchase, construct or otherwise acquire, maintain and keep in repair all things reasonable or convenient for the protection of the lands of the district from overflow and for the purpose of conserving or adding water to the sloughs and drains in the district.

(Added by Stats. 1959, Ch. 370.)

70151.
  

The district may co-operate and contract with the United States, the State of California, or any department or agency of either, in order to accomplish any of the purposes of the district.

(Added by Stats. 1959, Ch. 370.)
