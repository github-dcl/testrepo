Water Code - WAT

DIVISION 16. COUNTY WATERWORKS DISTRICTS [55000 - 55991]

( Division 16 added by Stats. 1953, Ch. 155. )

PART 4. FINANCIAL PROVISIONS [55500 - 55720]

( Part 4 added by Stats. 1953, Ch. 155. )

CHAPTER 2. Bonds [55520 - 55679]

( Chapter 2 added by Stats. 1953, Ch. 155. )

ARTICLE 4. Adoption of the Revenue Bond Law of 1941 [55610- 55610.]
( Heading of Article 4 amended by Stats. 1959, Ch. 1269. )

55610.

The Revenue Bond Law of 1941 is applicable to districts for the purpose of financing the production, transmission and distribution of water for public or private purposes.

(Amended by Stats. 1957, Ch. 53.)
