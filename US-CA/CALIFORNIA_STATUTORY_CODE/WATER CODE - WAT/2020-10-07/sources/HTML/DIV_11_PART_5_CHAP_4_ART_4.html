Water Code - WAT

DIVISION 11. IRRIGATION DISTRICTS [20500 - 29978]

( Division 11 added by Stats. 1943, Ch. 372. )

PART 5. POWERS AND PURPOSES [22075 - 22982]

( Part 5 added by Stats. 1943, Ch. 372. )

CHAPTER 4. Controversies [22650 - 22762]

( Chapter 4 added by Stats. 1943, Ch. 372. )

ARTICLE 4. Public Liability [22727- 22727.]
( Article 4 added by Stats. 1943, Ch. 372. )

22727.

All claims for money or damages against the district are governed by Part 3 (commencing with Section 900) and Part 4 (commencing with Section 940) of Division 3.6 of Title 1 of the Government Code except as provided therein, or by other statutes or regulations expressly applicable thereto.

(Amended by Stats. 1963, Ch. 1715.)
