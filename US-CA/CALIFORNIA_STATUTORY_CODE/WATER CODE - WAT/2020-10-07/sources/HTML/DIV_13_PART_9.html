Water Code - WAT

DIVISION 13. CALIFORNIA WATER DISTRICTS [34000 - 38501]

( Division 13 added by Stats. 1951, Ch. 390. )

PART 9. REPEALS [38500 - 38501]
( Part 9 added by Stats. 1951, Ch. 390. )

38500.

Chapter 387 of the Statutes of 1913 is hereby repealed.

(Added by Stats. 1951, Ch. 390.)

38501.

The repeal effected by this part shall not be construed to deprive any district or any person or other entity of any substantial right which would have existed or hereafter exist had such repeal not been effected.

(Added by Stats. 1951, Ch. 390.)
