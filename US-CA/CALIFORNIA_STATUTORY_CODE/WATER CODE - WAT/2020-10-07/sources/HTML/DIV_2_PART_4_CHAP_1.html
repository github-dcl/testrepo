Water Code - WAT

DIVISION 2. WATER [1000 - 5976]

( Division 2 enacted by Stats. 1943, Ch. 368. )

PART 4. DISTRIBUTION OF WATER IN WATERMASTER SERVICE AREAS [4000 - 4407]

( Part 4 enacted by Stats. 1943, Ch. 368. )

CHAPTER 1. General Provisions [4000 - 4003]
( Chapter 1 enacted by Stats. 1943, Ch. 368. )

4000.

As used in this part, service area applies to watermaster districts heretofore established and to watermaster service areas hereafter established pursuant to this part.

(Enacted by Stats. 1943, Ch. 368.)

4001.

As used in this part, conduit includes ditches, pipe lines, and other means of diversion.

(Enacted by Stats. 1943, Ch. 368.)

4002.

Any duty or authority vested in a watermaster may be exercised by the watermaster directly or through the agency of a deputy watermaster, if there is one.

(Enacted by Stats. 1943, Ch. 368.)

4003.

As used in this part, fiscal year means the year beginning July 1 of any calendar year and ending June 30 of the following calendar year.

(Added by Stats. 1961, Ch. 360.)
