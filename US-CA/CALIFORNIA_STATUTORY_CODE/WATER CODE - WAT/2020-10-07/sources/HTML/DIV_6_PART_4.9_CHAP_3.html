Water Code - WAT

DIVISION 6. CONSERVATION, DEVELOPMENT, AND UTILIZATION OF STATE WATER RESOURCES [10000 - 12999]

( Heading of Division 6 amended by Stats. 1957, Ch. 1932. )

PART 4.9. The Open and Transparent Water Data Act [12400 - 12420]

( Part 4.9 added by Stats. 2016, Ch. 506, Sec. 1. )

CHAPTER 3. Water Data Administration Fund [12420- 12420.]
( Chapter 3 added by Stats. 2016, Ch. 506, Sec. 1. )

12420.

(a)The Water Data Administration Fund is hereby created. All moneys in the fund are available, upon appropriation, to the department, the state board, or the Department of Fish and Wildlife for the collection, management, and improvement of water and ecological data for the purposes of this part.

(b)The Department of Finance shall develop a standardized agreement to allow for the voluntary donation to the fund by any person, educational institution, government entity, corporation or other business entity, or organization.

(Added by Stats. 2016, Ch. 506, Sec. 1. (AB 1755) Effective January 1, 2017.)
