Water Code - WAT

DIVISION 15. RECLAMATION DISTRICTS [50000 - 53901]

( Division 15 added by Stats. 1951, Ch. 336. )

PART 1. INTRODUCTORY PROVISIONS [50000 - 50170]

( Part 1 added by Stats. 1951, Ch. 336. )

CHAPTER 2. General Provisions [50100 - 50170]

( Chapter 2 added by Stats. 1951, Ch. 336. )

ARTICLE 4.5. Claims [50145- 50145.]
( Article 4.5 added by Stats. 1959, Ch. 1727. )

50145.

All claims for money or damages against the district are governed by Part 3 (commencing with Section 900) and Part 4 (commencing with Section 940) of Division 3.6 of Title 1 of the Government Code except as provided therein, or by other statutes or regulations expressly applicable thereto.

(Amended by Stats. 1963, Ch. 1715.)
