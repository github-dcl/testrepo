Water Code - WAT

DIVISION 15. RECLAMATION DISTRICTS [50000 - 53901]

  ( Division 15 added by Stats. 1951, Ch. 336. )

PART 10. CHANGES IN ORGANIZATION [53500 - 53805]

  ( Part 10 added by Stats. 1951, Ch. 336. )

CHAPTER 2. Change of Boundaries [53660- 53660.]

  ( Chapter 2 added by Stats. 1951, Ch. 336. )

ARTICLE 3. Inclusion [53660- 53660.]
  ( Article 3 added by Stats. 1951, Ch. 336. )

53660.
  

Land in compact form, capable of being embraced in a district, and not a part of another district, may be annexed to a district.

(Amended by Stats. 1965, Ch. 2043.)
