Water Code - WAT

DIVISION 13. CALIFORNIA WATER DISTRICTS [34000 - 38501]

( Division 13 added by Stats. 1951, Ch. 390. )

PART 3. INTERNAL ORGANIZATION [34700 - 34900]

( Part 3 added by Stats. 1951, Ch. 390. )

CHAPTER 3. The Secretary [34850 - 34851]
( Chapter 3 added by Stats. 1951, Ch. 390. )

34850.

The secretary shall keep a record of all the proceedings had at meetings of the board.

(Added by Stats. 1951, Ch. 390.)

34851.

All documents pertaining to district affairs shall be filed in the office of the district with the secretary and shall be open to inspection at all times by any person interested.

(Added by Stats. 1951, Ch. 390.)
