Water Code - WAT

DIVISION 12. COUNTY WATER DISTRICTS [30000 - 33901]

( Division 12 added by Stats. 1949, Ch. 274. )

PART 8. CHANGES IN ORGANIZATION [32200 - 32554]

( Part 8 added by Stats. 1949, Ch. 274. )

CHAPTER 2. Inclusion [32400- 32400.]
( Chapter 2 added by Stats. 1949, Ch. 274. )

32400.

Land not a part of the district whether or not contiguous to it or to other portions added to the district, and consisting of any portion of the county wherein the district was formed or of any municipality therein, or of land in any county contiguous to the county wherein the district was formed or of any municipality therein, may be included within the district.

(Added by Stats. 1949, Ch. 274.)
