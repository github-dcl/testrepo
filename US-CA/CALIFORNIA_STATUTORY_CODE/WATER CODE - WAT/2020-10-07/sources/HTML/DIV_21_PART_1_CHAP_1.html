Water Code - WAT

DIVISION 21. WATER CONSERVATION DISTRICTS [74000 - 76501]

( Division 21 added by Stats. 1965, Ch. 75. )

PART 1. INTRODUCTORY PROVISIONS [74000 - 74019]

( Part 1 added by Stats. 1965, Ch. 75. )

CHAPTER 1. Short Title and General Provisions [74000 - 74001]
( Chapter 1 added by Stats. 1965, Ch. 75. )

74000.

This division shall be known and may be cited as the Water Conservation District Law of 1931.

(Added by Stats. 1965, Ch. 75.)

74001.

This division shall be liberally construed to carry out the purposes and intent hereof.

(Added by Stats. 1965, Ch. 75.)
