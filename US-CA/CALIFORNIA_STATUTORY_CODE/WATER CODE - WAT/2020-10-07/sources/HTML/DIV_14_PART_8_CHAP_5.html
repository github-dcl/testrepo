Water Code - WAT

DIVISION 14. CALIFORNIA WATER STORAGE DISTRICT LAW [39000 - 48401]

  ( Division 14 added by Stats. 1951, Ch. 391. )

PART 8. BONDS [45100 - 45932]

  ( Part 8 added by Stats. 1951, Ch. 391. )

CHAPTER 5. Additional Bonds [45700 - 45702]
  ( Chapter 5 added by Stats. 1951, Ch. 391. )

45700.
  

If any district having authorized the issuance of any bonds shall issue additional bonds based on another assessment, the dates of maturity of the additional bonds or any series thereof shall be such that the latest maturity thereof shall not exceed 50 years from their date.

(Amended by Stats. 1967, Ch. 829.)

45702.
  

All provisions of this part relative to the original issue of bonds shall apply to any additional bonds.

(Amended by Stats. 1967, Ch. 829.)
