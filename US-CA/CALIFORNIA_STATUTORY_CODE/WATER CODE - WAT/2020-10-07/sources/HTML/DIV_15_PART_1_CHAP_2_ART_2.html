Water Code - WAT

DIVISION 15. RECLAMATION DISTRICTS [50000 - 53901]

  ( Division 15 added by Stats. 1951, Ch. 336. )

PART 1. INTRODUCTORY PROVISIONS [50000 - 50170]

  ( Part 1 added by Stats. 1951, Ch. 336. )

CHAPTER 2. General Provisions [50100 - 50170]

  ( Chapter 2 added by Stats. 1951, Ch. 336. )

ARTICLE 2. Districts Within Cities [50110 - 50111]
  ( Article 2 added by Stats. 1951, Ch. 336. )

50110.
  

Districts may be formed pursuant to this division for the reclamation of any land within any city, which land is subject in any manner, to overflow or incursions from the tide or inland waters of the State.

(Amended by Stats. 1951, Ch. 681.)

50111.
  

When districts are formed in cities pursuant to Section 50110 the duties required by this division to be performed by county officers shall be performed by the officers of the city who usually perform like duties.

(Added by Stats. 1951, Ch. 336.)
