Water Code - WAT

DIVISION 12. COUNTY WATER DISTRICTS [30000 - 33901]

( Division 12 added by Stats. 1949, Ch. 274. )

PART 5. POWERS AND PURPOSES [31000 - 31182]

( Part 5 added by Stats. 1949, Ch. 274. )

CHAPTER 2. Cooperation [31150 - 31182]

( Chapter 2 added by Stats. 1949, Ch. 274. )

ARTICLE 2. Water Service to Indian Land [31175- 31175.]
( Article 2 added by Stats. 1949, Ch. 274. )

31175.

A district may contract with the United States, upon such terms the board finds to be to the best interest of the district, for permanent or temporary service of water to Indian lands lying within the exterior boundaries of the district.

(Added by Stats. 1949, Ch. 274.)
