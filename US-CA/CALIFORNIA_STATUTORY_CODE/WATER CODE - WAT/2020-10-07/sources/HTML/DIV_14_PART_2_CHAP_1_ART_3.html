Water Code - WAT

DIVISION 14. CALIFORNIA WATER STORAGE DISTRICT LAW [39000 - 48401]

( Division 14 added by Stats. 1951, Ch. 391. )

PART 2. FORMATION [39400 - 40104]

( Part 2 added by Stats. 1951, Ch. 391. )

CHAPTER 1. Petition for Formation [39400 - 39466]

( Chapter 1 added by Stats. 1951, Ch. 391. )

ARTICLE 3. Time and Place of Hearing [39440- 39440.]
( Article 3 added by Stats. 1951, Ch. 391. )

39440.

Upon the presentation and filing of the formation petition and undertaking the department shall forthwith fix a time and place for the hearing of the petition. The place shall be either the office of the department at Sacramento or some place within an affected county. The time shall be not less than 30 nor more than 120 days after the presentation and filing of the petition.

(Amended by Stats. 1963, Ch. 369.)
