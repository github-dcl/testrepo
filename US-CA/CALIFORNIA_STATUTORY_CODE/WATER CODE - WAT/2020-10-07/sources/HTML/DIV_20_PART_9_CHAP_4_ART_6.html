Water Code - WAT

DIVISION 20. MUNICIPAL WATER DISTRICTS [71000 - 73001]

( Division 20 added by Stats. 1963, Ch. 156. )

PART 9. TAXES [72090 - 72146]

( Part 9 added by Stats. 1963, Ch. 156. )

CHAPTER 4. Chino Basin Production Assessments [72140 - 72146]

( Chapter 4 added by Stats. 1975, Ch. 165. )

ARTICLE 6. Remedies [72145- 72145.]
( Article 6 added by Stats. 1975, Ch. 165. )

72145.

Any assessing district may bring a suit in the court having jurisdiction against any producer for the collection of any delinquent production assessments, interest, or penalties. The court having jurisdiction of the suit may, in addition award interest, costs, and attorneys fees on any judgment.

(Added by Stats. 1975, Ch. 165.)
