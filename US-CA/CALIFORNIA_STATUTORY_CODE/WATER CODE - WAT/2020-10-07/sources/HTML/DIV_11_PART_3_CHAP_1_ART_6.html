Water Code - WAT

DIVISION 11. IRRIGATION DISTRICTS [20500 - 29978]

( Division 11 added by Stats. 1943, Ch. 372. )

PART 3. INTERNAL ORGANIZATION [21100 - 21405]

( Part 3 added by Stats. 1943, Ch. 372. )

CHAPTER 1. Officers [21100 - 21196]

( Chapter 1 added by Stats. 1943, Ch. 372. )

ARTICLE 6. Misconduct [21196- 21196.]
( Article 6 added by Stats. 1943, Ch. 372. )

21196.

For any wilful violation of any express duty herein provided for by any officer herein named he shall be subject to removal from office by proceedings brought in the superior court of the office county by any assessment payer of the district.

(Added by Stats. 1943, Ch. 372.)
