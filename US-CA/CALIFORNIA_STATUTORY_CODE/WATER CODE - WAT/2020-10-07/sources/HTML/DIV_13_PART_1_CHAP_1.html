DIVISION 13. CALIFORNIA WATER DISTRICTS [34000 - 38501]

  ( Division 13 added by Stats. 1951, Ch. 390. )

PART 1. INTRODUCTORY PROVISIONS [34000 - 34055]

  ( Part 1 added by Stats. 1951, Ch. 390. )

CHAPTER 1. Short Title [34000- 34000.]
  ( Chapter 1 added by Stats. 1951, Ch. 390. )

34000.
  

This division shall be known and may be cited as the California Water District Law.

(Added by Stats. 1951, Ch. 390.)
