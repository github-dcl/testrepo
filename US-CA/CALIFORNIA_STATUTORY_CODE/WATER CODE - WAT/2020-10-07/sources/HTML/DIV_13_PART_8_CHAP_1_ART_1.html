Water Code - WAT

DIVISION 13. CALIFORNIA WATER DISTRICTS [34000 - 38501]

( Division 13 added by Stats. 1951, Ch. 390. )

PART 8. CHANGES IN ORGANIZATION [37300 - 37580]

( Part 8 added by Stats. 1951, Ch. 390. )

CHAPTER 1. Exclusion [37300- 37300.]

( Chapter 1 added by Stats. 1951, Ch. 390. )

ARTICLE 1. Petition and Notice [37300- 37300.]
( Article 1 added by Stats. 1951, Ch. 390. )

37300.

One or more tracts of land may be excluded from the district under proceedings taken pursuant to the District Reorganization Act of 1965 (commencing at Section 56000, Government Code).

(Amended by Stats. 1965, Ch. 2043.)
