Water Code - WAT

DIVISION 5. FLOOD CONTROL [8000 - 9651]

( Division 5 added by Stats. 1943, Ch. 369. )

PART 5. RECLAMATION BOARD BOND ACT [9250 - 9577]

( Part 5 added by Stats. 1943, Ch. 369. )

CHAPTER 6. Bonds [9395 - 9527]

( Chapter 6 added by Stats. 1943, Ch. 369. )

ARTICLE 8. Bond Record [9505 - 9506]
( Article 8 added by Stats. 1943, Ch. 369. )

9505.

The board shall maintain in its office and open to public inspection at all reasonable times during office hours, a book or books to be known as the bond record of the drainage district, containing a complete record of the existing condition of the whole of each bond issued as compiled from time to time from reports from the State Treasurer, from which can be ascertained the amount of bonds outstanding and the interest accumulated and unpaid thereon.

(Added by Stats. 1943, Ch. 369.)

9506.

In case there are several bond issues under this part based upon several different assessments all of the proceedings, records and transactions of every kind provided for by this part shall be had and kept separately with reference to each bond issue.

(Added by Stats. 1943, Ch. 369.)
