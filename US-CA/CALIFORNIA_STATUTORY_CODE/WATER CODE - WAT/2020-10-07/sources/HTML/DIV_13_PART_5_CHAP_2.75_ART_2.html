Water Code - WAT

DIVISION 13. CALIFORNIA WATER DISTRICTS [34000 - 38501]

  ( Division 13 added by Stats. 1951, Ch. 390. )

PART 5. POWERS AND PURPOSES [35300 - 35886]

  ( Part 5 added by Stats. 1951, Ch. 390. )

CHAPTER 2.75. Provisions Pertaining Only to Lost Hills Water District [35540 - 35554]

  ( Chapter 2.75 added by Stats. 1974, Ch. 63. )

ARTICLE 2. Water Service Contracts [35554- 35554.]
  ( Article 2 added by Stats. 1989, Ch. 33, Sec. 3. )

35554.
  

An alternative to, and in addition to, other methods contained in this division regarding the apportionment of water, the district may enter into long-term water service contracts with the holders of title to land for the apportionment of all or any part of its water supply. Long-term water service contracts may provide that all water charges provided for, when due, are a lien on the land in the nature of assessments and may be collected and enforced in the manner provided in this division for the collection and enforcement of assessments. Any such lien shall have the same force, effect, and priority as an assessment lien if the contract is recorded in the office of the county recorder in the county in which the land is situated.

(Added by Stats. 1989, Ch. 33, Sec. 3. Effective May 31, 1989.)
