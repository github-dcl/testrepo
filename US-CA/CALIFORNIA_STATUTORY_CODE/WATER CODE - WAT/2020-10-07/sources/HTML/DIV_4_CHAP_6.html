Water Code - WAT

DIVISION 4. WELLS, PUMPING PLANTS, CONDUITS AND STREAMS [680.010 - 724.260]

  ( Division 4 enacted by Stats. 1943, Ch. 368. )

CHAPTER 6. Use of Streams as Conduits [7075- 7075.]
  ( Chapter 6 enacted by Stats. 1943, Ch. 368. )

7075.
  

Water which has been appropriated may be turned into the channel of another stream, mingled with its water, and then reclaimed; but in reclaiming it the water already appropriated by another shall not be diminished.

(Enacted by Stats. 1943, Ch. 368.)
