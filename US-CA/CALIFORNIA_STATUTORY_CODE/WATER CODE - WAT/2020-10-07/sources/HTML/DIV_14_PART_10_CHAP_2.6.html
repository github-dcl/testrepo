Water Code - WAT

DIVISION 14. CALIFORNIA WATER STORAGE DISTRICT LAW [39000 - 48401]

  ( Division 14 added by Stats. 1951, Ch. 391. )

PART 10. CHANGES IN ORGANIZATION [48000 - 48401]

  ( Part 10 added by Stats. 1951, Ch. 391. )

CHAPTER 2.6. Consolidation [48350- 48350.]
  ( Heading of Chapter 2.6 renumbered from Chapter 2.5 (as added by Stats. 1963, Ch. 163) by Stats. 1963, Ch. 2132. )

48350.
  

A district may be consolidated in the same manner as is provided for the consolidation of county water districts in Chapter 3 (commencing with Section 32650) of Part 8 of Division 12.

(Added by renumbering Section 48300 (as added by Stats. 1963, Ch. 163) by Stats. 1963, Ch. 2132.)
