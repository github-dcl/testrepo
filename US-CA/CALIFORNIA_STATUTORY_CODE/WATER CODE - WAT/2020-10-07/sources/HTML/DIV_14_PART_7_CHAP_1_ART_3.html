Water Code - WAT

DIVISION 14. CALIFORNIA WATER STORAGE DISTRICT LAW [39000 - 48401]

  ( Division 14 added by Stats. 1951, Ch. 391. )

PART 7. FINANCIAL ORGANIZATION [44400 - 44963]

  ( Part 7 added by Stats. 1951, Ch. 391. )

CHAPTER 1. General Financial Provisions [44400 - 44457]

  ( Chapter 1 added by Stats. 1951, Ch. 391. )

ARTICLE 3. Payment of Claims [44455 - 44457]
  ( Article 3 added by Stats. 1951, Ch. 391. )

44455.
  

All claims against the district shall be paid by warrants of the district.

(Added by Stats. 1951, Ch. 391.)

44456.
  

To provide a fund for payment of claims, the board may from time to time draw from the general fund deposited and kept to the credit of the district in the office of the county treasurer of a county having funds belonging to the district in his possession such sums as may be necessary. The sums shall be deposited with the treasurer of the district and paid out by him upon warrants of the district.

(Added by Stats. 1951, Ch. 391.)

44457.
  

All claims for money or damages against the district are governed by Part 3 (commencing with Section 900) and Part 4 (commencing with Section 940) of Division 3.6 of Title 1 of the Government Code except as provided therein, or by other statutes or regulations expressly applicable thereto.

(Amended by Stats. 1963, Ch. 1715.)
