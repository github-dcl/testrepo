Water Code - WAT

DIVISION 14. CALIFORNIA WATER STORAGE DISTRICT LAW [39000 - 48401]

( Division 14 added by Stats. 1951, Ch. 391. )

PART 3. INTERNAL ORGANIZATION [40300 - 40800]

( Part 3 added by Stats. 1951, Ch. 391. )

CHAPTER 4. Change of Office [40800- 40800.]
( Chapter 4 added by Stats. 1951, Ch. 391. )

40800.

The office of the board and its place of meetings may be changed by a majority vote of the board, but no such change shall become effective until after the resolution making it has been published once a week for two successive weeks in the county in which the office of the board has theretofore been located.

(Added by Stats. 1951, Ch. 391.)
