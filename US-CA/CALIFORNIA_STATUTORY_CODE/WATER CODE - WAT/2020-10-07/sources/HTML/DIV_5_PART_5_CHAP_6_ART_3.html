Water Code - WAT

DIVISION 5. FLOOD CONTROL [8000 - 9651]

( Division 5 added by Stats. 1943, Ch. 369. )

PART 5. RECLAMATION BOARD BOND ACT [9250 - 9577]

( Part 5 added by Stats. 1943, Ch. 369. )

CHAPTER 6. Bonds [9395 - 9527]

( Chapter 6 added by Stats. 1943, Ch. 369. )

ARTICLE 3. Validating Proceedings [9415- 9415.]
( Article 3 repealed and added by Stats. 1961, Ch. 1537. )

9415.

An action to determine the validity of bonds may be brought pursuant to Chapter 9 (commencing with Section 860) of Title 10 of Part 2 of the Code of Civil Procedure.

(Added by Stats. 1961, Ch. 1537.)
