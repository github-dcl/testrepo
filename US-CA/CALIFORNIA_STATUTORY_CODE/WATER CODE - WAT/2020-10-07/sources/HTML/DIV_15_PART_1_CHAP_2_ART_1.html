Water Code - WAT

DIVISION 15. RECLAMATION DISTRICTS [50000 - 53901]

( Division 15 added by Stats. 1951, Ch. 336. )

PART 1. INTRODUCTORY PROVISIONS [50000 - 50170]

( Part 1 added by Stats. 1951, Ch. 336. )

CHAPTER 2. General Provisions [50100 - 50170]

( Chapter 2 added by Stats. 1951, Ch. 336. )

ARTICLE 1. Applicability of Division [50100 - 50101]
( Article 1 added by Stats. 1951, Ch. 336. )

50100.

Nothing in this division impairs the validity of any district formed prior to the effective date of this division, its rights, or obligations.

(Added by Stats. 1951, Ch. 336.)

50101.

All swamp and overflowed salt-marsh and tidelands within one mile of the State Prison at San Quentin are excluded from the operation of this division.

(Amended by Stats. 1951, Ch. 681.)
