Water Code - WAT

DIVISION 12. COUNTY WATER DISTRICTS [30000 - 33901]

( Division 12 added by Stats. 1949, Ch. 274. )

PART 6. FINANCIAL PROVISIONS [31300 - 31639]

( Part 6 added by Stats. 1949, Ch. 274. )

CHAPTER 2. Bonds [31370 - 31482]

( Chapter 2 added by Stats. 1949, Ch. 274. )

ARTICLE 6. Callable Bonds [31460 - 31461]
( Article 6 added by Stats. 1949, Ch. 274. )

31460.

Any bonds issued by the district may be subject to call and redemption prior to maturity at such times and prices and upon such other terms as the board may specify.

(Amended by Stats. 1955, Ch. 1567.)

31461.

When bonds are made callable, a statement to that effect shall be set forth on the face of the bond.

(Added by Stats. 1949, Ch. 274.)
