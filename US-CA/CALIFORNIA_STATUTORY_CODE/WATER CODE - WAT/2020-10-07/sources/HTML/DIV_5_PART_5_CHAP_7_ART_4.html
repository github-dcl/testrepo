Water Code - WAT

DIVISION 5. FLOOD CONTROL [8000 - 9651]

  ( Division 5 added by Stats. 1943, Ch. 369. )

PART 5. RECLAMATION BOARD BOND ACT [9250 - 9577]

  ( Part 5 added by Stats. 1943, Ch. 369. )

CHAPTER 7. Sale of Land for Delinquent Installments [9535 - 9577]

  ( Chapter 7 added by Stats. 1943, Ch. 369. )

ARTICLE 4. Disposition of Proceeds [9575 - 9577]
  ( Article 4 added by Stats. 1943, Ch. 369. )

9575.
  

Out of the proceeds of a delinquent sale, made pursuant to the provisions of this chapter, the county treasurer shall transmit to the State Treasurer the amount due on the property so sold as shown in the notice, together with the penalty thereon.

(Added by Stats. 1943, Ch. 369.)

9576.
  

The State Treasurer shall place the amount to the credit of the bond fund of the drainage district for the particular bond issue upon the assessment.

(Added by Stats. 1943, Ch. 369.)

9577.
  

The county treasurer shall pay to the owner of the property any surplus remaining after the payment to the State Treasurer.

(Added by Stats. 1943, Ch. 369.)
