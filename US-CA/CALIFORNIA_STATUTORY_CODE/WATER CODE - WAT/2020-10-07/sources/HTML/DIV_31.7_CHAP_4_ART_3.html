Water Code - WAT

DIVISION 31.7. SAN FRANCISCO BAY AREA REGIONAL WATER SYSTEM FINANCING AUTHORITY [81600 - 81674]

( Division 31.7 added by Stats. 2002, Ch. 849, Sec. 1. )

CHAPTER 4. PowersandFunctionsofAuthority [81645 - 81662]

( Chapter 4 added by Stats. 2002, Ch. 849, Sec. 1. )

ARTICLE 3. Controversies [81660 - 81662]
( Article 3 added by Stats. 2002, Ch. 849, Sec. 1. )

81660.

The authority may sue and be sued in all actions and proceedings and in all courts and tribunals of competent jurisdiction.

(Added by Stats. 2002, Ch. 849, Sec. 1. Effective January 1, 2003.)

81661.

All claims for money or damages against the authority are governed by Part 3 (commencing with Section 900) and Part 4 (commencing with Section 940) of Division 3.6 of Title 1 of the Government Code.

(Added by Stats. 2002, Ch. 849, Sec. 1. Effective January 1, 2003.)

81662.

An action to determine the validity of any contract, bond, note, or other evidence of indebtedness may be brought pursuant to Chapter 9 (commencing with Section 860) of Title 10 of Part 2 of the Code of Civil Procedure.

(Added by Stats. 2002, Ch. 849, Sec. 1. Effective January 1, 2003.)
