Water Code - WAT

DIVISION 13. CALIFORNIA WATER DISTRICTS [34000 - 38501]

( Division 13 added by Stats. 1951, Ch. 390. )

PART 6.5. DISTRIBUTION DISTRICTS IN WATER DISTRICTS [36460 - 36543]

( Part 6.5 added by Stats. 1957, Ch. 1935. )

CHAPTER 3. Functioning of Distribution Districts [36520 - 36531]

( Chapter 3 added by Stats. 1957, Ch. 1935. )

ARTICLE 2. Actions and Proceedings [36530 - 36531]
( Article 2 added by Stats. 1957, Ch. 1935. )

36530.

All acts, proceedings, conclusions, and findings of fact, including the levy of assessments or charges, by a board of a district concerning a distribution district herein shall be conclusive except in an action or proceeding instituted within six months after the acts, proceedings, conclusions, or findings were had or made.

(Added by Stats. 1957, Ch. 1935.)

36531.

An action to determine the validity of an assessment may be brought pursuant to Chapter 9 (commencing with Section 860) of Title 10 of Part 2 of the Code of Civil Procedure.

(Amended by Stats. 1961, Ch. 1530.)
