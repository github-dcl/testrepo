Water Code - WAT

DIVISION 5. FLOOD CONTROL [8000 - 9651]

  ( Division 5 added by Stats. 1943, Ch. 369. )

PART 3. SACRAMENTO AND SAN JOAQUIN DRAINAGE DISTRICT [8500 - 8504]

  ( Part 3 added by Stats. 1943, Ch. 369. )

CHAPTER 1. Creation and Boundaries [8500 - 8504]
  ( Chapter 1 added by Stats. 1943, Ch. 369. )

8500.
  

The “Sacramento and San Joaquin Drainage District” is continued in existence.

(Added by Stats. 1943, Ch. 369.)

8501.
  

The boundaries of the district are set forth and described in Chapter 170, Statutes and Amendments to the Codes, California, 1913, and the description is hereby incorporated herein by reference as there set out.

(Added by Stats. 1943, Ch. 369.)

8502.
  

The management and control of the district are vested in the Central Valley Flood Protection Board.

(Amended by Stats. 2009, Ch. 275, Sec. 2. (AB 1165) Effective January 1, 2010.)

8503.
  

The district is a body corporate and politic and may sue and be sued.

(Added by Stats. 1943, Ch. 369.)

8504.
  

The district may acquire, own, hold, use, and enjoy any and all properties necessary for the purposes of the district.

(Added by Stats. 1943, Ch. 369.)
