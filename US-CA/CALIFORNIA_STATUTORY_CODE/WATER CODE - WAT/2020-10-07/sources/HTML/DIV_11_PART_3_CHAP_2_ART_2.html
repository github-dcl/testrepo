Water Code - WAT

DIVISION 11. IRRIGATION DISTRICTS [20500 - 29978]

  ( Division 11 added by Stats. 1943, Ch. 372. )

PART 3. INTERNAL ORGANIZATION [21100 - 21405]

  ( Part 3 added by Stats. 1943, Ch. 372. )

CHAPTER 2. Selecting Officers in Certain Cases [21265 - 21285]

  ( Chapter 2 added by Stats. 1943, Ch. 372. )

ARTICLE 2. Appointments in Certain Districts [21285- 21285.]
  ( Article 2 added by Stats. 1968, Ch. 246. )

21285.
  

Notwithstanding any other provision of law, in any district where there are not more than 15 voters at such time as the publication of notice of a general district election, no such general district election shall be held, and all offices shall be filled by appointment pursuant to Section 10515 of the Elections Code.

(Amended by Stats. 1994, Ch. 923, Sec. 227. Effective January 1, 1995.)
