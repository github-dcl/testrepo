Water Code - WAT

DIVISION 13. CALIFORNIA WATER DISTRICTS [34000 - 38501]

( Division 13 added by Stats. 1951, Ch. 390. )

PART 6. FINANCIAL PROVISIONS [35900 - 36458.1]

( Part 6 added by Stats. 1951, Ch. 390. )

CHAPTER 3.5. Alternate Provisions for the Issuance of General Obligation Bonds [36250 - 36262]

( Chapter 3.5 added by Stats. 1970, Ch. 1458. )

ARTICLE 1. General [36250- 36250.]
( Article 1 added by Stats. 1970, Ch. 1458. )

36250.

The provisions of this chapter are in addition to any and all other provisions of this division, the California Water District Law, and any other applicable laws for the issuance of general obligation bonds by a district or an improvement district thereof.

(Added by Stats. 1970, Ch. 1458.)
