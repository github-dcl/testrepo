Water Code - WAT

DIVISION 12. COUNTY WATER DISTRICTS [30000 - 33901]

( Division 12 added by Stats. 1949, Ch. 274. )

PART 7. TAXES [31650 - 31822]

( Part 7 added by Stats. 1949, Ch. 274. )

[CHAPTER 3. Alternative Tax Procedure ] [31805 - 31822]

( Chapter 3 added by Stats. 1949, Ch. 274. )

[ARTICLE 6. Collection by the Assessor] [31822- 31822.]
( Article 6 (commencing with Section 31820) added by Stats. 1949, Ch. 274. )

31822.

If the assessee owns taxable real property in fee in the district, the taxes due upon the possession of, claim to, or right to the possession of land are respectively a lien upon that real property, which lien attaches as of the first day of March in each year. The taxes need not be collected by the assessor if in his opinion the taxable real property is sufficient to secure the payment of the taxes.

(Added by Stats. 1968, Ch. 89. Applicable, by Sec. 6 of Ch. 89, to districts operating under former Chapter 3 pursuant to Stats. 1967, Ch. 592, Sec. 8. Note: Chapter 3, commencing with Section 31730, was repealed Nov. 8, 1967, by Stats. 1967, Ch. 592.)
