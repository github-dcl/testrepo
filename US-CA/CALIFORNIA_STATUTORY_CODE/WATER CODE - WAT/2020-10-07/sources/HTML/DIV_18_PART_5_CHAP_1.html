Water Code - WAT

DIVISION 18. WATER REPLENISHMENT DISTRICTS [60000 - 60622]

( Division 18 added by Stats. 1955, Ch. 1514. )

PART 5. FINANCES [60240 - 60292]

( Part 5 added by Stats. 1955, Ch. 1514. )

CHAPTER 1. Depositary [60240- 60240.]
( Chapter 1 added by Stats. 1955, Ch. 1514. )

60240.

Any money belonging to a district may be deposited or invested and drawn out as provided in Title 5, Division 2, Part 1, Chapter 4, Article 2 of the Government Code, as now or hereafter amended.

(Added by Stats. 1955, Ch. 1514.)
