Water Code - WAT

DIVISION 15. RECLAMATION DISTRICTS [50000 - 53901]

( Division 15 added by Stats. 1951, Ch. 336. )

PART 8. BONDS AND REFUNDING BONDS [52100 - 52862]

( Part 8 added by Stats. 1951, Ch. 336. )

CHAPTER 1. General Provisions [52100 - 52193]

( Chapter 1 added by Stats. 1951, Ch. 336. )

ARTICLE 3. Sale of Bonds [52150 - 52155]
( Article 3 added by Stats. 1951, Ch. 336. )

52150.

The county treasurer shall publish notice of the sale of bonds, at least once a week for two weeks prior to the sale, in a newspaper of general circulation published in the principal county.

(Added by Stats. 1951, Ch. 336.)

52151.

The notice shall state that the county treasurer will sell a specified amount of bonds at the time and place specified therein, and ask sealed proposals for the purchase of the bonds, or any part thereof.

(Added by Stats. 1951, Ch. 336.)

52152.

At the time stated in the notice of sale, the county treasurer shall open the bids and award the bonds to the highest responsible bidder as provided in Article 2, Chapter 2 of this part with respect to the sale of bonds or Article 3, Chapter 3 of this part with respect to the sale of refunding bonds.

(Added by Stats. 1951, Ch. 336.)

52153.

The date fixed for the sale of refunding bonds shall be at least 95 days prior to the maturity date of the principal of the bonds to be refunded by the sale of the refunding bonds.

(Added by Stats. 1951, Ch. 336.)

52154.

The county treasurer may, and upon written request of a majority of the board, shall reject any and all bids.

(Added by Stats. 1951, Ch. 336.)

52155.

A record of the sale shall be made upon the books of the county treasurer.

(Added by Stats. 1951, Ch. 336.)
