Water Code - WAT

DIVISION 13. CALIFORNIA WATER DISTRICTS [34000 - 38501]

  ( Division 13 added by Stats. 1951, Ch. 390. )

PART 6. FINANCIAL PROVISIONS [35900 - 36458.1]

  ( Part 6 added by Stats. 1951, Ch. 390. )

CHAPTER 2. Provisions Relating to All Bonds [35950 - 36064]

  ( Chapter 2 added by Stats. 1951, Ch. 390. )

ARTICLE 2. Lien of Bonds [35975- 35975.]
  ( Article 2 added by Stats. 1951, Ch. 390. )

35975.
  

Unless otherwise provided in the proceedings for the issuance of the bonds, they and the interest on them shall be paid from an annual assessment upon land or charges which in the discretion of the board are fixed and collected in lieu thereof and all land shall be and remain liable to the assessed for these payments.

(Amended by Stats. 1957, Ch. 53.)
