Water Code - WAT

DIVISION 20. MUNICIPAL WATER DISTRICTS [71000 - 73001]

( Division 20 added by Stats. 1963, Ch. 156. )

PART 7. BONDS [71852 - 71996]

( Part 7 added by Stats. 1963, Ch. 156. )

CHAPTER 7. Formation of Improvement Districts for Issuance of Revenue Bonds [71980 - 71996]

( Chapter 7 added by Stats. 1975, Ch. 229. )

ARTICLE 5. Advance of Funds [71995 - 71996]
( Article 5 added by Stats. 1975, Ch. 229. )

71995.

The board may advance general funds of the district to accomplish the purposes of a revenue improvement district formed pursuant to this chapter.

(Added by Stats. 1975, Ch. 229.)

71996.

The board may repay the district for any advance of funds from the proceeds of the sale of revenue bonds authorized for the purposes of the improvement district.

(Added by Stats. 1975, Ch. 229.)
