Water Code - WAT

DIVISION 12. COUNTY WATER DISTRICTS [30000 - 33901]

( Division 12 added by Stats. 1949, Ch. 274. )

PART 8. CHANGES IN ORGANIZATION [32200 - 32554]

( Part 8 added by Stats. 1949, Ch. 274. )

CHAPTER 1. Exclusion [32200- 32200.]
( Chapter 1 added by Stats. 1949, Ch. 274. )

32200.

Any tract of land included within any district and not substantially and directly benefited by being in the district, or by its continued inclusion therein, may be excluded from the district.

(Amended by Stats. 1965, Ch. 2043.)
