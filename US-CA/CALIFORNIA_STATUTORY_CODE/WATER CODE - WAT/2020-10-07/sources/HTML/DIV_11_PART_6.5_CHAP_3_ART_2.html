Water Code - WAT

DIVISION 11. IRRIGATION DISTRICTS [20500 - 29978]

( Division 11 added by Stats. 1943, Ch. 372. )

PART 6.5. DISTRIBUTION DISTRICTS IN IRRIGATION DISTRICTS [23500 - 23583]

( Part 6.5 added by Stats. 1949, Ch. 169. )

CHAPTER 3. Functioning of Distribution Districts [23550 - 23571]

( Chapter 3 added by Stats. 1949, Ch. 169. )

ARTICLE 2. Distribution District Warrants [23560- 23560.]
( Article 2 added by Stats. 1949, Ch. 169. )

23560.

A district may issue distribution district warrants signed by its president and secretary from receipts of distribution district assessments and charges in such amounts and to the United States or such persons as may be required to carry out the provisions of a contract between a distribution district and the United States, executed under the federal reclamation laws.

(Added by Stats. 1949, Ch. 169.)
