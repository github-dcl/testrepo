Water Code - WAT

DIVISION 2. WATER [1000 - 5976]

( Division 2 enacted by Stats. 1943, Ch. 368. )

PART 2. APPROPRIATION OF WATER [1200 - 1851]

( Part 2 enacted by Stats. 1943, Ch. 368. )

CHAPTER 12. Enforcement of Water Rights [1825 - 1851]

( Chapter 12 added by Stats. 1980, Ch. 933, Sec. 13. )

ARTICLE 1. Policy [1825- 1825.]
( Article 1 added by Stats. 1980, Ch. 933, Sec. 13. )

1825.

It is the intent of the Legislature that the state take vigorous action to enforce the terms and conditions of permits, licenses, certifications, and registrations to appropriate water, to enforce state board orders and decisions, and to prevent the unlawful diversion of water.

(Amended by Stats. 2018, Ch. 92, Sec. 215. (SB 1289) Effective January 1, 2019.)
