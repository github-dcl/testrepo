Water Code - WAT

DIVISION 26. SAFE DRINKING WATER, CLEAN WATER, WATERSHED PROTECTION, AND FLOOD PROTECTION ACT [79000 - 79222]

  ( Division 26 added by Stats. 1999, Ch. 725, Sec. 1. )

CHAPTER 7. Clean Water and Water Recycling Program [79105 - 79149.16]

  ( Chapter 7 added by Stats. 1999, Ch. 725, Sec. 1. )

ARTICLE 1. Clean Water and Water Recycling Account [79105 - 79106]
  ( Article 1 added by Stats. 1999, Ch. 725, Sec. 1. )

79105.
  

For the purposes of this chapter, “account” means the Clean Water and Water Recycling Account created by Section 79106.

(Added by Stats. 1999, Ch. 725, Sec. 1. Approved in Proposition 13 at the March 7, 2000, election.)

79106.
  

The Clean Water and Water Recycling Account is hereby created in the fund. The sum of three hundred fifty-five million dollars ($355,000,000) is hereby transferred from the fund to the account.

(Amended by Stats. 2000, Ch. 1078, Sec. 6. Effective January 1, 2001. Note: This section was added by Stats. 1999, Ch. 725, and approved in Prop. 13 on March 7, 2000.)
