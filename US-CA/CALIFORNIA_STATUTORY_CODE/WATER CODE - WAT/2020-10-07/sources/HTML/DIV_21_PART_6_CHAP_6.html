Water Code - WAT

DIVISION 21. WATER CONSERVATION DISTRICTS [74000 - 76501]

  ( Division 21 added by Stats. 1965, Ch. 75. )

PART 6. FINANCIAL PROVISIONS [74750 - 74977]

  ( Part 6 added by Stats. 1965, Ch. 75. )

CHAPTER 6. Issuance of Revenue Bonds Pursuant to the Revenue Bond Law of 1941 [74975 - 74977]
  ( Chapter 6 added by Stats. 1971, Ch. 359. )

74975.
  

Subject to the limitations of this chapter, revenue bonds, including refunding revenue bonds, may be issued by a district under the Revenue Bond Law of 1941, Chapter 6 (commencing with Section 54300), Part 1, Division 2, Title 5 of the Government Code.

(Added by Stats. 1971, Ch. 359.)

74976.
  

This chapter provides an alternative authority and procedure for the subject to which it relates, but does not affect any other law relating to such subject or a similar subject. When proceeding under the provisions of this chapter, such provisions alone need be followed.

(Added by Stats. 1971, Ch. 359.)

74977.
  

For the purposes of this chapter, the term “enterprise,” as used in the Revenue Bond Law of 1941, shall be limited to and shall include only those works or property authorized to be acquired, constructed, improved or financed by a water conservation district pursuant to this division or any other applicable provision of law.

(Added by Stats. 1971, Ch. 359.)
