Water Code - WAT

DIVISION 12. COUNTY WATER DISTRICTS [30000 - 33901]

  ( Division 12 added by Stats. 1949, Ch. 274. )

PART 6. FINANCIAL PROVISIONS [31300 - 31639]

  ( Part 6 added by Stats. 1949, Ch. 274. )

CHAPTER 5. Alternative Procedure for Formation of Improvement Districts [31585 - 31618]

  ( Chapter 5 added by Stats. 1959, Ch. 442. )

ARTICLE 1. In General [31585 - 31586]
  ( Article 1 added by Stats. 1959, Ch. 442. )

31585.
  

The provisions of this chapter are in addition to the provisions of Sections 31388 and 31389, the provisions of Sections 31500 to 31548, inclusive, and the provisions of Sections 31575 to 31577, inclusive.

(Added by Stats. 1959, Ch. 442.)

31586.
  

Division 4 (commencing at Section 2800) of the Streets and Highways Code shall not be applicable to the formation of any improvement district pursuant to this chapter.

(Added by Stats. 1959, Ch. 442.)
