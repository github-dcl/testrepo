Water Code - WAT

DIVISION 13. CALIFORNIA WATER DISTRICTS [34000 - 38501]

( Division 13 added by Stats. 1951, Ch. 390. )

PART 6. FINANCIAL PROVISIONS [35900 - 36458.1]

( Part 6 added by Stats. 1951, Ch. 390. )

CHAPTER 2. Provisions Relating to All Bonds [35950 - 36064]

( Chapter 2 added by Stats. 1951, Ch. 390. )

ARTICLE 5. Validating Proceedings [36050- 36050.]
( Article 5 repealed and added by Stats. 1961, Ch. 1486. )

36050.

An action to determine the validity of bonds may be brought pursuant to Chapter 9 (commencing with Section 860) of Title 10 of Part 2 of the Code of Civil Procedure.

(Repealed and added by Stats. 1961, Ch. 1486.)
