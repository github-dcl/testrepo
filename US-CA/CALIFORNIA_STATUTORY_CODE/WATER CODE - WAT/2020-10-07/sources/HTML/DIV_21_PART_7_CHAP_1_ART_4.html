Water Code - WAT

DIVISION 21. WATER CONSERVATION DISTRICTS [74000 - 76501]

  ( Division 21 added by Stats. 1965, Ch. 75. )

PART 7. IMPROVEMENT DISTRICTS [75000 - 75231]

  ( Part 7 added by Stats. 1965, Ch. 75. )

CHAPTER 1. Improvement Districts for Issuance of Bonds [75000 - 75090]

  ( Chapter 1 added by Stats. 1965, Ch. 75. )

ARTICLE 4. Notice of Bond Election [75050 - 75051]
  ( Article 4 added by Stats. 1965, Ch. 75. )

75050.
  

Notice shall be given of the bond election by publication in a newspaper published in each of the counties in which the improvement district is situated, if there is one, at least once a week for three weeks previous to the election, by posting notice thereof in three public places in the improvement district, and by posting a copy in the office of the district.

(Added by Stats. 1965, Ch. 75.)

75051.
  

The notice of the bond election shall designate a name for the improvement district and describe the boundaries thereof. The notice shall also designate the respective election precincts and the polling place in each, the election officers, the time of the election, and the hours during which the polls will be kept open; provided, that the polls shall be opened not later than 8 o'clock a.m. and kept open until 7 o'clock p.m.

(Added by Stats. 1965, Ch. 75.)
