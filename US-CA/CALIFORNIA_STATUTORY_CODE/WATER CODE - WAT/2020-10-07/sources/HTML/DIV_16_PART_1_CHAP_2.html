Water Code - WAT

DIVISION 16. COUNTY WATERWORKS DISTRICTS [55000 - 55991]

  ( Division 16 added by Stats. 1953, Ch. 155. )

PART 1. INTRODUCTORY PROVISIONS [55000 - 55031]

  ( Part 1 added by Stats. 1953, Ch. 155. )

CHAPTER 2. Definitions [55010 - 55014]
  ( Chapter 2 added by Stats. 1953, Ch. 155. )

55010.
  

Unless the context otherwise requires, the provisions of this chapter shall govern the construction of this division.

(Added by Stats. 1953, Ch. 155.)

55011.
  

The definition of a word applies to any of its variants.

(Added by Stats. 1953, Ch. 155.)

55012.
  

“District” means any county irrigation district or county waterworks district formed pursuant to the County Waterworks District Act or any county waterworks district formed pursuant to this division.

(Added by Stats. 1953, Ch. 155.)

55013.
  

“Board” means the governing body of a district.

(Added by Stats. 1953, Ch. 155.)

55014.
  

“County” means the county within which the district or proposed district is located.

(Added by Stats. 1953, Ch. 155.)
