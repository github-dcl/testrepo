Water Code - WAT

DIVISION 1. GENERAL STATE POWERS OVER WATER [100 - 540]

( Division 1 enacted by Stats. 1943, Ch. 368. )

CHAPTER 3.7. Water Standbyand Availability Charges [389- 389.]
( Chapter 3.7 added by Stats. 1992, Ch. 778, Sec. 1. )

389.

(a)Notwithstanding any other provision of law, the term water standby charge and the term water availability charge have the same meaning.

(b)Subdivision (a) does not constitute a change in, but is declaratory of, existing law.

(Added by Stats. 1992, Ch. 778, Sec. 1. Effective September 21, 1992.)
