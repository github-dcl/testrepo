Water Code - WAT

DIVISION 3. DAMS AND RESERVOIRS [6000 - 6612]

( Heading of Division 3 amended by Stats. 1965, Ch. 1225. )

PART 1. SUPERVISION OF DAMS AND RESERVOIRS [6000 - 6470]

( Heading of Part 1 amended by Stats. 1965, Ch. 1225. )

CHAPTER 7. Inspection and Approval [6350 - 6407]

( Chapter 7 enacted by Stats. 1943, Ch. 368. )

ARTICLE 2. Repaired or Altered Dams and Reservoirs [6360 - 6364]
( Heading of Article 2 amended by Stats. 1965, Ch. 1225. )

6360.

Immediately upon completion of the repair or alteration of any dam or reservoir, the owner shall give notice of completion to the department and as soon thereafter as possible shall file with it supplementary drawings or descriptive matter showing or describing the dam or reservoir as actually repaired or altered together with such maps, data, records, and information pertaining to the dam or reservoir as repaired or altered as the department requires.

(Amended by Stats. 1965, Ch. 1225.)

6362.

As soon as practicable the dam or reservoir as repaired or altered shall be inspected by the department.

(Amended by Stats. 1965, Ch. 1225.)

6363.

A certificate of approval shall be issued upon a finding that the dam or reservoir is safe to impound water within the limitations prescribed in the certificate. Pending issuance of a new certificate of approval, the owner of the dam or reservoir shall not, through action or inaction, cause the dam or reservoir to impound water beyond the limitations prescribed in the existing certificate.

(Amended by Stats. 1965, Ch. 1225.)

6364.

The certificate of approval shall supersede any previous certificate of approval issued for the dam or reservoir so repaired or altered.

(Amended by Stats. 1965, Ch. 1225.)
