Water Code - WAT

DIVISION 13. CALIFORNIA WATER DISTRICTS [34000 - 38501]

( Division 13 added by Stats. 1951, Ch. 390. )

PART 8. CHANGES IN ORGANIZATION [37300 - 37580]

( Part 8 added by Stats. 1951, Ch. 390. )

CHAPTER 2. Inclusion [37485 - 37580]

( Chapter 2 added by Stats. 1951, Ch. 390. )

ARTICLE 1. Petition and Notice [37485- 37485.]
( Article 1 added by Stats. 1951, Ch. 390. )

37485.

Land not a part of the district whether or not contiguous to it may be included within the district.

(Added by Stats. 1951, Ch. 390.)
