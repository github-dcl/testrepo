Water Code - WAT

DIVISION 5. FLOOD CONTROL [8000 - 9651]

( Division 5 added by Stats. 1943, Ch. 369. )

PART 4. THE CENTRAL VALLEY FLOOD PROTECTION BOARD [8520 - 9142]

( Heading of Part 4 amended by Stats. 2007, Ch. 366, Sec. 6. )

CHAPTER 2. Administrative Provisions [8550 - 8585]

( Chapter 2 added by Stats. 1943, Ch. 369. )

ARTICLE 2. Employees [8580- 8580.]
( Article 2 repealed and added by Stats. 2007, Ch. 366, Sec. 19. )

8580.

(a)The board may appoint an executive officer.

(b)The board may appoint a chief engineer.

(c)The board may employ legal counsel and other necessary staff.

(Repealed and added by Stats. 2007, Ch. 366, Sec. 19. Effective January 1, 2008.)
