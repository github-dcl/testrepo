Water Code - WAT

DIVISION 21. WATER CONSERVATION DISTRICTS [74000 - 76501]

( Division 21 added by Stats. 1965, Ch. 75. )

PART 10. CHANGES IN ORGANIZATION [75750 - 76061]

( Part 10 added by Stats. 1965, Ch. 75. )

CHAPTER 3. Annexation of Districts [75940 - 76010]

( Chapter 3 added by Stats. 1965, Ch. 75. )

ARTICLE 1. Definitions [75940- 75940.]
( Article 1 added by Stats. 1965, Ch. 75. )

75940.

As used in this chapter:

(a)Petitioning district means a water conservation district which proposes to petition or which has petitioned another water conservation district to annex its territory to that other water conservation district.

(b)Annexing district means a water conservation district which has been petitioned by a petitioning district to be annexed thereto.

(Added by Stats. 1965, Ch. 75.)
