Water Code - WAT

DIVISION 2. WATER [1000 - 5976]

( Division 2 enacted by Stats. 1943, Ch. 368. )

PART 4. DISTRIBUTION OF WATER IN WATERMASTER SERVICE AREAS [4000 - 4407]

( Part 4 enacted by Stats. 1943, Ch. 368. )

CHAPTER 8. The Water Resources Revolving Fund [4351 - 4358]
( Heading of Chapter 8 amended by Stats. 1957, Ch. 1411. )

4351.

Separate accounts shall be established in the fund to the credit of the various service areas now in existence or hereafter created from time to time.

(Amended by Stats. 1957, Ch. 1411.)

4357.

The expenditures for the supervision of the distribution of water in any service area shall be paid from that portion or account of the Water Resources Revolving Fund credited on the departments books to that service area, upon claims approved by the department and otherwise audited and approved as may be required in the case of other claims against the state.

(Amended by Stats. 2004, Ch. 230, Sec. 30. Effective August 16, 2004.)

4358.

Any money remaining in the watermaster service area accounts of the Water Resources Revolving Fund at the end of any year shall be available for use the following year for the respective service areas to which credited.

(Amended by Stats. 1957, Ch. 1411.)
