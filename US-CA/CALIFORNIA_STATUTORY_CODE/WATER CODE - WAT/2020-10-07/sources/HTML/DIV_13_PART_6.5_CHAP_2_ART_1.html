Water Code - WAT

DIVISION 13. CALIFORNIA WATER DISTRICTS [34000 - 38501]

  ( Division 13 added by Stats. 1951, Ch. 390. )

PART 6.5. DISTRIBUTION DISTRICTS IN WATER DISTRICTS [36460 - 36543]

  ( Part 6.5 added by Stats. 1957, Ch. 1935. )

CHAPTER 2. Inclusion and Exclusion of Land [36500 - 36514]

  ( Chapter 2 added by Stats. 1957, Ch. 1935. )

ARTICLE 1. Inclusion of Land [36500 - 36502]
  ( Article 1 added by Stats. 1957, Ch. 1935. )

36500.
  

If at any time it is desired to include additional land in a distribution district, a petition for inclusion signed by the owners of the land to be included and by two-thirds in number of the holders of title to the land in the distribution district may be filed with the board.

(Added by Stats. 1957, Ch. 1935.)

36501.
  

The inclusion petition shall describe the land in the distribution district as enlarged by the proposed inclusions and give the names and addresses of the owners of the additional land in substantially the same manner as in the original petition for forming a distribution district.

(Added by Stats. 1957, Ch. 1935.)

36502.
  

The same proceedings shall be had on the distribution district inclusion petition as upon the original petition for the formation of a distribution district.

(Added by Stats. 1957, Ch. 1935.)
