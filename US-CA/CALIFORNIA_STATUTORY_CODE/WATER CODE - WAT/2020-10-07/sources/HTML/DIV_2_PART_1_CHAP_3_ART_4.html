Water Code - WAT

DIVISION 2. WATER [1000 - 5976]

  ( Division 2 enacted by Stats. 1943, Ch. 368. )

PART 1. GENERAL PROVISIONS [1000 - 1126]

  ( Part 1 enacted by Stats. 1943, Ch. 368. )

CHAPTER 3. Witnesses and Production of Evidence [1075 - 1106]

  ( Chapter 3 enacted by Stats. 1943, Ch. 368. )

ARTICLE 4. Depositions [1100- 1100.]
  ( Article 4 enacted by Stats. 1943, Ch. 368. )

1100.
  

The board or any party to a proceeding before it may, in any investigation or hearing, cause the deposition of witnesses residing within or without the state to be taken in the manner prescribed by law for depositions in civil actions in the superior courts of this state under Title 4 (commencing with Section 2016.010) of Part 4 of the Code of Civil Procedure.

(Amended by Stats. 2004, Ch. 182, Sec. 60. Effective January 1, 2005. Operative July 1, 2005, by Sec. 64 of Ch. 182.)
