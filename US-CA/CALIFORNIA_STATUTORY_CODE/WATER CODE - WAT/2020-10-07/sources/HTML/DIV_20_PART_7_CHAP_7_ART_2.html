Water Code - WAT

DIVISION 20. MUNICIPAL WATER DISTRICTS [71000 - 73001]

( Division 20 added by Stats. 1963, Ch. 156. )

PART 7. BONDS [71852 - 71996]

( Part 7 added by Stats. 1963, Ch. 156. )

CHAPTER 7. Formation of Improvement Districts for Issuance of Revenue Bonds [71980 - 71996]

( Chapter 7 added by Stats. 1975, Ch. 229. )

ARTICLE 2. Hearing and Changes [71986- 71986.]
( Article 2 added by Stats. 1975, Ch. 229. )

71986.

Except as otherwise provided in this article, the board shall proceed with the hearing, conduct the hearing, make changes to the enterprise or the estimated amount of the proposed debt and hold hearings on the changes pursuant to Sections 71880 to 71883, inclusive.

(Added by Stats. 1975, Ch. 229.)
