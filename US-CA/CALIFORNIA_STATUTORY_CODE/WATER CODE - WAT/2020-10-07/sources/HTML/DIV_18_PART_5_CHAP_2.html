Water Code - WAT

DIVISION 18. WATER REPLENISHMENT DISTRICTS [60000 - 60622]

  ( Division 18 added by Stats. 1955, Ch. 1514. )

PART 5. FINANCES [60240 - 60292]

  ( Part 5 added by Stats. 1955, Ch. 1514. )

CHAPTER 2. Water Charge [60245 - 60246]
  ( Chapter 2 added by Stats. 1955, Ch. 1514. )

60245.
  

The board shall fix such rate or rates for the sale or exchange of water for replenishment purposes only as will result in revenues which will pay, insofar as practicable, the operating expenses of the district.

(Added by Stats. 1955, Ch. 1514.)

60246.
  

The rate charged shall be uniform for like classes and conditions of service.

(Added by Stats. 1955, Ch. 1514.)
