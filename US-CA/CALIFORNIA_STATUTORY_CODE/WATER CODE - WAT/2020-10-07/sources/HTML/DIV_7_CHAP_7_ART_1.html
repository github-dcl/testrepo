Water Code - WAT

DIVISION 7. WATER QUALITY [13000 - 16104]

  ( Division 7 repealed and added by Stats. 1969, Ch. 482. )

CHAPTER 7. Water Reclamation [13500 - 13558.1]

  ( Chapter 7 added by Stats. 1969, Ch. 482. )

ARTICLE 1. Short Title [13500- 13500.]
  ( Article 1 added by Stats. 1969, Ch. 482. )

13500.
  

This chapter shall be known as and may be cited as the Water Recycling Law.

(Amended by Stats. 1995, Ch. 28, Sec. 25. Effective January 1, 1996.)
