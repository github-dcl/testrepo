Water Code - WAT

DIVISION 26. SAFE DRINKING WATER, CLEAN WATER, WATERSHED PROTECTION, AND FLOOD PROTECTION ACT [79000 - 79222]

( Division 26 added by Stats. 1999, Ch. 725, Sec. 1. )

CHAPTER 6. WatershedProtectionProgram [79070 - 79104.206]

( Chapter 6 added by Stats. 1999, Ch. 725, Sec. 1. )

ARTICLE 1. Watershed Protection Account [79070 - 79071]
( Article 1 added by Stats. 1999, Ch. 725, Sec. 1. )

79070.

For the purposes of this chapter, account means the Watershed Protection Account created by Section 79071.

(Added by Stats. 1999, Ch. 725, Sec. 1. Approved in Proposition 13 at the March 7, 2000, election.)

79071.

The Watershed Protection Account is hereby created in the fund. The sum of four hundred sixty-eight million dollars ($468,000,000) is hereby transferred from the fund to the account.

(Added by Stats. 1999, Ch. 725, Sec. 1. Approved in Proposition 13 at the March 7, 2000, election.)
