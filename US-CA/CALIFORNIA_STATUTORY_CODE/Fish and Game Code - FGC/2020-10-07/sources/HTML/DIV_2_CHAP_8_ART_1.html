Fish and Game Code - FGC

DIVISION 2. DEPARTMENT OF FISH AND WILDLIFE [700 - 1940]

( Heading of Division 2 amended by Stats. 2015, Ch. 154, Sec. 21. )

CHAPTER 8. Conservation of Wildlife Resources [1800 - 1802]

( Chapter 8 added by Stats. 1974, Ch. 1190. )

ARTICLE 1. Definitions [1800- 1800.]
( Article 1 added by Stats. 1974, Ch. 1190. )

1800.

As used in this chapter wildlife means birds, mammals, and reptiles not raised in captivity.

(Added by Stats. 1974, Ch. 1190.)
