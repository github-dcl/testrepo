Fish and Game Code - FGC

DIVISION 2. DEPARTMENT OF FISH AND WILDLIFE [700 - 1940]

  ( Heading of Division 2 amended by Stats. 2015, Ch. 154, Sec. 21. )

CHAPTER 7.5. Native Species Conservation and Enhancement [1750 - 1773.2]

  ( Heading of Chapter 7.5 renumbered from Chapter 8 (as added by Stats. 1974, Ch. 898) by Stats. 1976, Ch. 1079. )

ARTICLE 1. Short Title [1750- 1750.]
  ( Article 1 added by Stats. 1974, Ch. 898. )

1750.
  

This chapter shall be known and may be cited as the Native Species Conservation and Enhancement Act.

(Added by Stats. 1974, Ch. 898.)
