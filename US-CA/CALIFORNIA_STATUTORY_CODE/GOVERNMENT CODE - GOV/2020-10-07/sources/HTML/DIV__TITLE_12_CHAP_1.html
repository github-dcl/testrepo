TITLE 12. NORTH COAST RAILROAD AUTHORITY [93000 - 93025]

  ( Title 12 added by Stats. 1989, Ch. 1085, Sec. 1. )

CHAPTER 1. General Provisions [93000 - 93005]
  ( Chapter 1 added by Stats. 1989, Ch. 1085, Sec. 1. )

93000.
  

This title shall be known and may be cited as the North Coast Railroad Authority Closure and Transition to Trails Act.

(Amended by Stats. 2018, Ch. 934, Sec. 3. (SB 1029) Effective January 1, 2019.)

93003.
  

The Legislature finds and declares that it is in the public interest to dissolve the authority, and to transfer its rights-of-way to other entities for the purpose of potentially developing a trail that could include railbanking and continuing freight where it was operational on January 1, 2018.

(Repealed and added by Stats. 2018, Ch. 934, Sec. 7. (SB 1029) Effective January 1, 2019.)

93004.
  

As used in this title “authority” means the North Coast Railroad Authority.

(Added by Stats. 1989, Ch. 1085, Sec. 1.)

93005.
  

The authority is a local agency for purposes of the Disaster Assistance Act (Chapter 7.5 (commencing with Section 8680) of Division 1 of Title 2).

(Amended by Stats. 2002, Ch. 461, Sec. 18. Effective January 1, 2003.)
