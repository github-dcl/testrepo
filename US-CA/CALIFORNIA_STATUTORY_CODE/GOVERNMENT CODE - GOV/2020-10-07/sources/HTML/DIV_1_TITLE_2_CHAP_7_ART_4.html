TITLE 2. GOVERNMENT OF THE STATE OF CALIFORNIA [8000 - 22980]

( Title 2 enacted by Stats. 1943, Ch. 134. )

DIVISION 1. GENERAL [8000 - 8899.72]

( Division 1 enacted by Stats. 1943, Ch. 134. )

CHAPTER 7. California Emergency Services Act [8550 - 8669.7]

( Chapter 7 added by Stats. 1970, Ch. 1454. )

ARTICLE 4. California Emergency Council [8575- 8575.]
( Article 4 added by Stats. 1970, Ch. 1454. )

8575.

For the purposes of the California Disaster and Civil Defense Master Mutual Aid Agreement, the Office of Emergency Services will serve as the State Disaster Council.

(Amended by Stats. 2013, Ch. 352, Sec. 117. (AB 1317) Effective September 26, 2013. Operative July 1, 2013, by Sec. 543 of Ch. 352.)
