TITLE 1. GENERAL [100 - 7914]

  ( Title 1 enacted by Stats. 1943, Ch. 134. )

DIVISION 7. MISCELLANEOUS [6000 - 7599.2]

  ( Division 7 enacted by Stats. 1943, Ch. 134. )

CHAPTER 31. Public Offices Hours [7595- 7595.]
  ( Chapter 31 added by Stats. 1990, Ch. 1506, Sec. 1. )

7595.
  

All state agencies which provide over-the-counter information and services directly to the public shall provide those services during the customary lunch hours of 11:30 a.m. to 1:30 p.m. on those days that the offices are open to the public. This section shall not apply to any state office which has three or less full-time employees who are not peace officers at an office on any single day.

(Added by Stats. 1990, Ch. 1506, Sec. 1.)
