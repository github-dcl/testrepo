TITLE 8. THE ORGANIZATION AND GOVERNMENT OF COURTS [68070 - 77655]

( Title 8 added by Stats. 1953, Ch. 206. )

CHAPTER 10. Other Municipal Courts Districts [73301 - 74988]

( Chapter 10 added by Stats. 1953, Ch. 206. )

ARTICLE 12. Marin County [73770 - 73771]
( Article 12 repealed and added by Stats. 2002, Ch. 784, Sec. 429. )

73770.

This article applies to the judicial district of the Marin County Municipal Court.

(Repealed and added by Stats. 2002, Ch. 784, Sec. 429. Effective January 1, 2003.)

73771.

A branch court shall be maintained at an appropriate location in the former Western Judicial District.

(Repealed and added by Stats. 2002, Ch. 784, Sec. 429. Effective January 1, 2003.)
