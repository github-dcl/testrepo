TITLE 2. GOVERNMENT OF THE STATE OF CALIFORNIA [8000 - 22980]

  ( Title 2 enacted by Stats. 1943, Ch. 134. )

DIVISION 4. FISCAL AFFAIRS [16100 - 17700]

  ( Division 4 added by Stats. 1945, Ch. 119. )

PART 1. FUNDS FOR SUBVENTIONS [16100 - 16202]

  ( Part 1 added by Stats. 1969, Ch. 1526. )

CHAPTER 4. In-Lieu Property Tax Relief for Recipients of Public Assistance [16160- 16160.]
  ( Chapter 4 added by Stats. 1972, Ch. 1406. )

16160.
  

Out of the amount appropriated to the Controller by Section 16100, he shall expend the sums required by Section 15204.1 of the Welfare and Institutions Code.

(Added by Stats. 1972, Ch. 1406.)
