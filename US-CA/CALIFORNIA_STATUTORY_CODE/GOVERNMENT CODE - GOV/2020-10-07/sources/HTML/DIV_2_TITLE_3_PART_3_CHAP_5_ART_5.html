TITLE 3. GOVERNMENT OF COUNTIES [23000 - 33205]

( Title 3 added by Stats. 1947, Ch. 424. )

DIVISION 2. OFFICERS [24000 - 28085]

( Division 2 added by Stats. 1947, Ch. 424. )

PART 3. OTHER OFFICERS [26500 - 27773]

( Part 3 added by Stats. 1947, Ch. 424. )

CHAPTER 5. County Treasurer [27000 - 27137]

( Chapter 5 added by Stats. 1947, Ch. 424. )

ARTICLE 5. Vacancy [27120 - 27121]
( Article 5 added by Stats. 1947, Ch. 424. )

27120.

Whenever an action based upon official misconduct is commenced against the county treasurer, the board of supervisors may suspend him from office until the suit is determined. The board may appoint some person to fill the vacancy, who shall qualify and give such bond as the board determines.

(Added by Stats. 1947, Ch. 424.)

27121.

In case of the death of the county treasurer, his legal representatives shall deliver to the person appointed to fill the vacancy all official money, books, accounts, papers, and documents which are or come into their possession.

(Added by Stats. 1947, Ch. 424.)
