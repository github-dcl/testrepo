Government Code - GOV

TITLE 1. GENERAL [100 - 7914]

( Title 1 enacted by Stats. 1943, Ch. 134. )

DIVISION 7. MISCELLANEOUS [6000 - 7599.2]

( Division 7 enacted by Stats. 1943, Ch. 134. )

CHAPTER 1. Publications and Official Advertising [6000 - 6078]

( Chapter 1 enacted by Stats. 1943, Ch. 134. )

ARTICLE 5. Foreign Language Newspapers [6075 - 6078]
( Article 5 added by Stats. 1953, Ch. 170. )

6075.

As used in this article, newspaper includes weekly newspaper, magazine, periodical or other publication.

(Added by Stats. 1953, Ch. 170.)

6076.

The publisher or owner of any newspaper published in the foreign language of any enemy nation shall file with the district attorney of the county in which it is printed or distributed a correct translation in English of every article, statement, or word published in the newpaper.

(Added by Stats. 1953, Ch. 170.)

6077.

Upon request the publisher shall furnish to the Attorney General or to the district attorney of the county where the newspaper is published a translation of any article, statement, or word published in the newspaper. The translation shall be certified as correct by one competent to make a translation.

(Added by Stats. 1953, Ch. 170.)

6078.

Any person violating this article is guilty of a misdemeanor.

(Added by Stats. 1953, Ch. 170.)
