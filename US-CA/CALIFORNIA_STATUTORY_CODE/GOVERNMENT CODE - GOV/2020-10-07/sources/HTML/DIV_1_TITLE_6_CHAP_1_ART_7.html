TITLE 6. DISTRICTS [58000 - 62262]

( Title 6 added by Stats. 1951, Ch. 331. )

DIVISION 1. GENERAL [58000 - 60375.5]

( Heading of Division 1 renumbered from Division 2 by Stats. 1987, Ch. 56, Sec. 88. )

CHAPTER 1. The District Organization Law [58000 - 58200]

( Chapter 1 added by Stats. 1951, Ch. 331. )

ARTICLE 7. Validating Proceedings [58200- 58200.]
( Article 7 repealed and added by Stats. 1961, Ch. 1552. )

58200.

An action to determine the legality of the existence of the district may be brought pursuant to Chapter 9 (commencing with Section 860) of Title 10 of Part 2 of the Code of Civil Procedure.

(Repealed and added by Stats. 1961, Ch. 1552.)
