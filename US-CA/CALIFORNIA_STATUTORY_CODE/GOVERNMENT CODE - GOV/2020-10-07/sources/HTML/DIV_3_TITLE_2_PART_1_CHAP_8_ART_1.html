TITLE 2. GOVERNMENT OF THE STATE OF CALIFORNIA [8000 - 22980]

( Title 2 enacted by Stats. 1943, Ch. 134. )

DIVISION 3. EXECUTIVE DEPARTMENT [11000 - 15986]

( Division 3 added by Stats. 1945, Ch. 111. )

PART 1. STATE DEPARTMENTS AND AGENCIES [11000 - 11898]

( Part 1 added by Stats. 1945, Ch. 111. )

CHAPTER 8. The Performanceand Results Actof 1993 [11800 - 11801]

( Chapter 8 added by Stats. 1993, Ch. 641, Sec. 1. )

ARTICLE 1. General Provisions [11800 - 11801]
( Article 1 added by Stats. 1993, Ch. 641, Sec. 1. )

11800.

This chapter shall be known and may be cited as the Performance and Results Act of 1993.

(Added by Stats. 1993, Ch. 641, Sec. 1. Effective October 1, 1993.)

11801.

The Legislature finds and declares the following:

(a)The Legislature has an interest in improving the delivery of governmental services through the use of strategic planning and performance measurement.

(b)Strategic plans and budget contracts will provide managerial accountability and flexibility to state agencies and departments that participate in a performance budgeting pilot program.

(Added by Stats. 1993, Ch. 641, Sec. 1. Effective October 1, 1993.)
