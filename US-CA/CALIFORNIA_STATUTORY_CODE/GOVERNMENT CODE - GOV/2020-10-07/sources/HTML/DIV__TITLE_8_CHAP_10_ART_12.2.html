TITLE 8. THE ORGANIZATION AND GOVERNMENT OF COURTS [68070 - 77655]

  ( Title 8 added by Stats. 1953, Ch. 206. )

CHAPTER 10. Other Municipal Courts Districts [73301 - 74988]

  ( Chapter 10 added by Stats. 1953, Ch. 206. )

ARTICLE 12.2. Mariposa County [73783.1 - 73783.3]
  ( Article 12.2 repealed and added by Stats. 2002, Ch. 784, Sec. 431. )

73783.1.
  

This article applies to the municipal court established in a judicial district embracing the County of Mariposa.

(Repealed and added by Stats. 2002, Ch. 784, Sec. 431. Effective January 1, 2003.)

73783.3.
  

Facilities for the court shall be maintained at the county seat and at court facilities provided elsewhere as determined by ordinance adopted by the board of supervisors. The court shall determine the nature and frequency of sessions held at additional court locations designated by the board of supervisors. Jurors shall be drawn from the entire county.

(Repealed and added by Stats. 2002, Ch. 784, Sec. 431. Effective January 1, 2003.)
