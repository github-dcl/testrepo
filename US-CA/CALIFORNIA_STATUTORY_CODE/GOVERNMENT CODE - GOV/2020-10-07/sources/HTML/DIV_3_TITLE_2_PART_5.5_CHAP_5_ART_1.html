TITLE 2. GOVERNMENT OF THE STATE OF CALIFORNIA [8000 - 22980]

  ( Title 2 enacted by Stats. 1943, Ch. 134. )

DIVISION 3. EXECUTIVE DEPARTMENT [11000 - 15986]

  ( Division 3 added by Stats. 1945, Ch. 111. )

PART 5.5. DEPARTMENT OF GENERAL SERVICES [14600 - 14985.11]

  ( Part 5.5 added by Stats. 1965, Ch. 371. )

CHAPTER 5. State Records [14740 - 14746]

  ( Chapter 5 added by Stats. 1965, Ch. 371. )

ARTICLE 1. General [14740 - 14741.1]
  ( Article 1 added by Stats. 1965, Ch. 371. )

14740.
  

This chapter shall be known as the State Records Storage Act.

(Amended by Stats. 2014, Ch. 28, Sec. 31. (SB 854) Effective June 20, 2014.)

14741.
  

As used in this chapter “record” or “records” means all papers, maps, exhibits, magnetic or paper tapes, photographic films and prints, punched cards, and other documents produced, received, owned or used by an agency, regardless of physical form or characteristics. Library and museum materials made or acquired and preserved solely for reference or exhibition purposes, and stocks of publications and of processed documents are not included within the definition of the term “record” or “records” as used in this chapter.

(Added by Stats. 1965, Ch. 371.)

14741.1.
  

As used in this chapter, “public use forms” means those forms used by the state to obtain or to solicit facts, opinions, or other information from the public or private citizens, partnerships, corporations, organizations, business trusts, or any nongovernmental entity or legal representative thereof.

(Added by Stats. 1982, Ch. 1118, Sec. 2.)
