Government Code - GOV

TITLE 4. GOVERNMENT OF CITIES [34000 - 45345]

  ( Title 4 added by Stats. 1949, Ch. 79. )

DIVISION 4. FINANCIAL PROVISIONS [43000 - 43904]

  ( Division 4 added by Stats. 1949, Ch. 79. )

CHAPTER 3. Funds [43400 - 43455]

  ( Chapter 3 added by Stats. 1949, Ch. 79. )

ARTICLE 1. General [43400 - 43401]
  ( Article 1 added by Stats. 1949, Ch. 79. )

43400.
  

All money received from licenses, street poll taxes, fines, penalties, and forfeitures shall be paid into the general fund.

(Added by Stats. 1949, Ch. 79.)

43401.
  

All moneys received from sales and use taxes shall be paid into the General Fund, or into such special fund or funds for such purpose or purposes as shall be established by ordinance.

(Added by Stats. 1959, Ch. 767.)
