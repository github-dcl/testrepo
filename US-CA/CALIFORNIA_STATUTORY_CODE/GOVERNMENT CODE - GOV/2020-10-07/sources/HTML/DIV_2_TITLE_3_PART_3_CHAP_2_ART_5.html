TITLE 3. GOVERNMENT OF COUNTIES [23000 - 33205]

( Title 3 added by Stats. 1947, Ch. 424. )

DIVISION 2. OFFICERS [24000 - 28085]

( Division 2 added by Stats. 1947, Ch. 424. )

PART 3. OTHER OFFICERS [26500 - 27773]

( Part 3 added by Stats. 1947, Ch. 424. )

CHAPTER 2. Sheriff [26600 - 26778]

( Chapter 2 added by Stats. 1947, Ch. 424. )

ARTICLE 5. Badges [26690- 26690.]
( Article 5 added by Stats. 1947, Ch. 424. )

26690.

The board of supervisors shall furnish the sheriff and deputy sheriffs with suitable badges of office. Upon the badge of the sheriff shall be inscribed Sheriff, and upon those of the deputy sheriffs shall be inscribed Deputy Sheriff.

(Added by Stats. 1947, Ch. 424.)
