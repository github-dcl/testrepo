TITLE 2. GOVERNMENT OF THE STATE OF CALIFORNIA [8000 - 22980]

( Title 2 enacted by Stats. 1943, Ch. 134. )

DIVISION 3. EXECUTIVE DEPARTMENT [11000 - 15986]

( Division 3 added by Stats. 1945, Ch. 111. )

PART 1. STATE DEPARTMENTS AND AGENCIES [11000 - 11898]

( Part 1 added by Stats. 1945, Ch. 111. )

CHAPTER 8.1. State Government Strategic Planningand Performance Review Act [11810 - 11817]

( Chapter 8.1 added by Stats. 1994, Ch. 779, Sec. 1. )

ARTICLE 1. General Provisions [11810- 11810.]
( Article 1 added by Stats. 1994, Ch. 779, Sec. 1. )

11810.

This chapter shall be known, and may be cited, as the State Government Strategic Planning and Performance and Review Act.

(Added by Stats. 1994, Ch. 779, Sec. 1. Effective September 26, 1994.)
