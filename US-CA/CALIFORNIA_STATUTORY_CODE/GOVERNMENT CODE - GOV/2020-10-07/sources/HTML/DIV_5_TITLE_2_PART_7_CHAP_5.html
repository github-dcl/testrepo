TITLE 2. GOVERNMENT OF THE STATE OF CALIFORNIA [8000 - 22980]

( Title 2 enacted by Stats. 1943, Ch. 134. )

DIVISION 5. PERSONNEL [18000 - 22980]

( Division 5 added by Stats. 1945, Ch. 123. )

PART 7. STATE PEACE OFFICERS' AND FIREFIGHTERS' DEFINED CONTRIBUTION PLAN [22960 - 22960.99]

( Part 7 added by Stats. 1998, Ch. 820, Sec. 11. )

CHAPTER 5. Eligibility [22960.55- 22960.55.]
( Chapter 5 added by Stats. 1998, Ch. 820, Sec. 11. )

22960.55.

(a)Any person who is an eligible employee on the effective date of the plan, as set forth in the memorandum of understanding, shall become a participant on the effective date of the plan.

(b)Any person who becomes an eligible employee after the effective date of the plan shall become a participant on the first day of covered employment.

(Added by Stats. 1998, Ch. 820, Sec. 11. Effective September 25, 1998.)
