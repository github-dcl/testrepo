TITLE 1. GENERAL [100 - 7914]

  ( Title 1 enacted by Stats. 1943, Ch. 134. )

DIVISION 7. MISCELLANEOUS [6000 - 7599.2]

  ( Division 7 enacted by Stats. 1943, Ch. 134. )

CHAPTER 5.5. Local Agency Self-Insurance Authority [6599.01 - 6599.41]

  ( Chapter 5.5 added by Stats. 1986, Ch. 1327, Sec. 1. )

ARTICLE 4. Miscellaneous [6599.41- 6599.41.]
  ( Article 4 added by Stats. 1986, Ch. 1327, Sec. 1. )

6599.41.
  

If the original board is not selected on or before January 1, 1990, this chapter shall cease to be operative.

(Added by Stats. 1986, Ch. 1327, Sec. 1. Note: Condition for termination of Chapter 5.5 (commencing with Section 6599.01) failed.)
