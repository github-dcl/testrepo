Government Code - GOV

TITLE 1. GENERAL [100 - 7914]

  ( Title 1 enacted by Stats. 1943, Ch. 134. )

DIVISION 5. PUBLIC WORK AND PUBLIC PURCHASES [4000 - 4563]

  ( Division 5 enacted by Stats. 1943, Ch. 134. )

CHAPTER 5. Emergency Termination of Public Contracts [4400 - 4412]

  ( Chapter 5 added by Stats. 1949, Ch. 1105. )

ARTICLE 1. General Provisions [4400 - 4401]
  ( Article 1 added by Stats. 1949, Ch. 1105. )

4400.
  

This chapter shall be known and cited as the “Emergency Termination of Public Contracts Act.”

(Added by Stats. 1949, Ch. 1105.)

4401.
  

“Public agency,” as used in this chapter, includes the State, its various commissions, boards and departments and any county, city, district or state agency authorized to enter into contracts for public work.

(Added by Stats. 1949, Ch. 1105.)
