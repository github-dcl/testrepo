9-802. Savings clause.

(a) Except as otherwise provided in this part, this article applies to a transaction or lien within its scope, even if the transaction or lien was entered into or created before July 1, 2013.

(b) This article as amended by Laws 2011, LB90, does not affect an action, case, or proceeding commenced before July 1, 2013.
