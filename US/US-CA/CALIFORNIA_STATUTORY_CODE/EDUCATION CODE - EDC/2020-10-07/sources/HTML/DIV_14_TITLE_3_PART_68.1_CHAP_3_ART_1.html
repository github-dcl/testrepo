Education Code - EDC

TITLE 3. POSTSECONDARY EDUCATION [66000 - 101060]

( Title 3 enacted by Stats. 1976, Ch. 1010. )

DIVISION 14. MISCELLANEOUS [99000 - 101149.5]

( Division 14 enacted by Stats. 1976, Ch. 1010. )

PART 68.1. KINDERGARTENUNIVERSITY PUBLIC EDUCATION FACILITIES BOND ACT OF 2002 [100600 - 100760]

( Part 68.1 added by Stats. 2002, Ch. 33, Sec. 30. )

CHAPTER 3. HigherEducationFacilities [100650 - 100760]

( Chapter 3 added by Stats. 2002, Ch. 33, Sec. 30. )

ARTICLE 1. General [100650- 100650.]
( Article 1 added by Stats. 2002, Ch. 33, Sec. 30. )

100650.

(a)The system of public higher education in this state includes the University of California, the Hastings College of the Law, the California State University, the California Community Colleges, and their respective off-campus centers.

(b)The 2002 Higher Education Capital Outlay Bond Fund is hereby established in the State Treasury for deposit of funds from the proceeds of bonds issued and sold for the purposes of this chapter.

(c)The Higher Education Facilities Finance Committee established pursuant to Section 67353 is hereby authorized to create a debt or debts, liability or liabilities, of the State of California pursuant to this chapter for the purpose of providing funds to aid the University of California, the Hastings College of the Law, the California State University, and the California Community Colleges.

(Added by Stats. 2002, Ch. 33, Sec. 30. Approved in Proposition 47 at the November 5, 2002, election.)
