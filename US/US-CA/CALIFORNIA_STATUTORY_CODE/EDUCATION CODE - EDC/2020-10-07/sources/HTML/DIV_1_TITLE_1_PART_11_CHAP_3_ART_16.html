PART 11. LIBRARIES [18010 - 20092]

  ( Part 11 enacted by Stats. 1976, Ch. 1010. )

CHAPTER 3. Unified School Districts and Union High School Districts Public Libraries [18300 - 18571]

  ( Chapter 3 enacted by Stats. 1976, Ch. 1010. )

ARTICLE 16. Payment of Interest and Bonds [18570 - 18571]
  ( Article 16 enacted by Stats. 1976, Ch. 1010. )

18570.
  

The principal and interest on the bonds shall be paid by the county treasurer, upon the warrant of the county auditor, out of the fund provided therefor.

(Enacted by Stats. 1976, Ch. 1010.)

18571.
  

The county auditor shall cancel and file with the county treasurer the bonds and coupons as rapidly as they are paid.

(Enacted by Stats. 1976, Ch. 1010.)
