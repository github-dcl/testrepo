Education Code - EDC

TITLE 3. POSTSECONDARY EDUCATION [66000 - 101060]

( Title 3 enacted by Stats. 1976, Ch. 1010. )

DIVISION 10. PRIVATE POSTSECONDARY AND HIGHER EDUCATION INSTITUTIONS [94000 - 95010]

( Division 10 enacted by Stats. 1976, Ch. 1010. )

PART 59. PRIVATE POSTSECONDARY AND HIGHER EDUCATION INSTITUTIONS [94000 - 95010]

( Part 59 enacted by Stats. 1976, Ch. 1010. )

CHAPTER 5. Eminent Domain [94500- 94500.]
( Chapter 5 enacted by Stats. 1976, Ch. 1010. )

94500.

Any educational institution of collegiate grade within this state not conducted for profit may acquire by eminent domain any property necessary to carry out any of its powers or functions.

(Enacted by Stats. 1976, Ch. 1010.)
