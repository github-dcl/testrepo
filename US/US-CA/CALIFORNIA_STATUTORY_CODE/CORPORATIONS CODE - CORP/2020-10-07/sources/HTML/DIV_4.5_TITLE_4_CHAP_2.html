Corporations Code - CORP

TITLE 4. SECURITIES [25000 - 31516]

  ( Title 4 added by Stats. 1949, Ch. 384. )

DIVISION 4.5. COMMODITIES [29500 - 29567]

  ( Division 4.5 added by Stats. 1990, Ch. 969, Sec. 2. )

CHAPTER 2. Unlawful Commodity Transactions [29520- 29520.]
  ( Chapter 2 added by Stats. 1990, Ch. 969, Sec. 2. )

29520.
  

Except as otherwise provided in Section 29530, 29531, or 29532, no person shall sell or purchase or offer to sell or purchase any commodity under any commodity contract or under any commodity option, or offer to enter into, or enter into, as seller or purchaser any commodity contract or any commodity option.

(Added by Stats. 1990, Ch. 969, Sec. 2.)
